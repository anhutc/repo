The head is selected to be exchanged.

Prepare for exchange and shut down

Heads selected for exchange return all nozzles. The relevant gantries are moved to the setup position and, on certain machine types, the component tables are undocked.
A dialog box then opens. Here you must confirm that you want to shut down the entire machine by clicking Shut down.
