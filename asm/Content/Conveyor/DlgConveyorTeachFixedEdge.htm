Teaching the position of the left/right rail

Move left

Every click of the button moves the conveyor rail one step of the selected step size to the left (viewed from the direction of transport).

Move right

Every click of the button moves the conveyor rail one step of the selected step size to the right (viewed from the direction of transport).

Step

Select the desired step size with which to move the conveyor rail.

OK

Closes the dialog box. The position that has been taught is accepted.

Cancel

Closes the dialog box without saving the position that has been taught.
