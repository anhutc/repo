Fixed conveyor rails

Only one of the two conveyor rails is ever moved when the conveyor width is adjusted. The rail which does not move is referred to as the "fixed conveyor rail".

Left

When viewed from the direction of transport, the fixed conveyor rail of the board conveyor is on the left.

Right

When viewed from the direction of transport, the fixed conveyor rail of the board conveyor is on the right.

Outer

The fixed conveyor rail is on the right for conveyor lane 1 and on the left for conveyor lane 2 (when viewed from the direction of transport).

User-defined

The position of the fixed conveyor rail can be freely defined within the predefined value range.

Extended conveyor configuration

Dual

Both conveyor lanes are used.

Dual as single

Only one conveyor lane is used and is widened. The other lane is deactivated. The maximum width of the active conveyor lane can thus be set to 450 mm. If the fixed conveyor rails are on the right or on the outside, the right-hand conveyor lane is widened. If the fixed conveyor rails are on the left, the left-hand conveyor lane is widened.

Note: In the case of dual conveyors with bolted fixed rails, the "Dual as single" option is only available if the bolted fixed rails are on the outside.
