Teach WPTC alignment position

 

The end of the rail of the relevant feed axis is used as the adjustment position to allow the WPTC alignment position to be set correctly for both towers

  1. Select the feed axis of the relevant tower.
  2. Press Teach WPTC alignment position and then Next.
  3. Insert a waffle pack tray carrier in the bottom level of cassette 1 and confirm that you have done this by pressing Next. The tower is moved to the transfer position for cassette 1 and level 1 is provided.
  4. Open the cover to release the feed axis. Align the outer edge of the tray carrier with the end of the feed axis rail.
  5. Press Finish to store the current position in the MTC's internal machine data.

Caution: The WPTC alignment position is calculated on the basis of this position:

Tower 1: Current position + 3 mm

Tower 2: Current position - 6 mm
