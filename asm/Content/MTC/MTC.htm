Matrix tray changer - MTC

The use of a Matrix Tray Changer (MTC) allows you to make available a greater range of flatpack ICs.

A matrix tray changer comprises 2 towers. Tower 1 has 5 cassettes, each cassette can in turn contain up to 6 waffle pack tray carriers, tower 2 has 4 cassettes with a maximum of 10 waffle pack tray carriers. Both towers can be operated independently of each other. This allows, for example, components to be picked up from one tower while the other tower is being refilled.
