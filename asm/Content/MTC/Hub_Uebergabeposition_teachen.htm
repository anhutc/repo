Teach transfer position

 

  1. Press Teach transfer position and then Next.
  2. Press the button for the cassette whose transfer position is to be taught and confirm your selection by pressing Next.
  3. Insert a waffle pack tray carrier in the required level and confirm that you have done this by pressing Next.
  4. If necessary, teach the transfer position using the and buttons. (Every press of these buttons moves the lifting axis one step of the selected step size in the corresponding direction.)
  5. To check whether the transfer position has been set correctly, the waffle pack tray carrier must be unlocked. Do this by pressing Open WPTC lock. It is now possible to push the waffle pack tray carrier onto the slide rail.
  6. To close the lock again, press OK.
  7. Press Finish to store the current position as the transfer position for the selected cassette in the internal machine data for the MTC or WPC.
