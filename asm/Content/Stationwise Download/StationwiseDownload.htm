Change job by station

To be able to obtain the shortest possible product change times, it is possible to specify a new job without having to empty a complete line.

This is done separately for each station:

If the last board of the current job is loaded into a station, the operator at the station must initiate the job change. The current station is emptied. Then the changeover can be performed and once the changeover process has been confirmed, the new job can be processed.

1. Station:

A new recipe is specified at the SIPLACE Pro Line Control system.
The operator must press this icon when the last board of the previous job (A) moves into the machine.
Caution: If the operator fails to do this at the right time, this may lead to incorrect placement!

All other stations:

The last board of the previous job (A) has been moved out of the conveyor of the upstream station.
The operator must click this icon when the last board moves onto the input section.

The operator has already confirmed that the last board of the current job (A) has moved in. If this was confirmed at the wrong time, confirmation can be canceled within a period of 10 seconds by pressing the button again.

In SIPLACE Pro, it has been specified that each time a board moves into the input section, the operator will be asked whether this is the last board of job A or the first board of job B.

The input section is locked:

The board in the input section is not the last board of job A.

The last board of job A has been confirmed, the input section is locked. Job B is not yet active.

Boards of job B are being moved in and can be produced. They remain in the output section, however, until job B is also active on the downstream station.
