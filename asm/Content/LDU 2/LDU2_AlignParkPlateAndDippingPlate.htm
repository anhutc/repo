Align park plate and dip plate

Prerequisite: The dip plate and park plate must be fitted, but the flux tank must not be present.

Proceed as follows to align the dip plate with the park plate using the manual operations:

  1. Mount the linear dipping unit LDU 2 as specified.

  2. Switch to the view Manual operations > X-Table , select the dip module (LDU 2) and press Details...

  3. Press Align park plate and dip plate.

  4. First choose an increment.

  5. Then press or to move the dip plate up or down by this amount.

  6. Press Accept to save the set height when the two plates are at exactly the same height.
