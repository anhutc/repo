Dismantling the linear dipping unit (LDU 2) / cleaning the LDU 2

The linear dipping unit must be dismantled to allow the component parts to be cleaned.

To dismantle the linear dipping unit (LDU 2), proceed as follows:

  1. Switch to the view Setup > X-Table > , select the dip module and press Settings...

  2. Select the Cleaning tab.

  3. Press 1. Unhook flux tank. The flux tank is moved onto the dipping plate, the dipping plate axis is moved down and the squeegee axis is moved back.

  4. Push the flux tank into the middle of the dipping plate.

  5. Make sure that the placement head is not above the LDU 2.

  6. Press 2. Remove dip plate.

  7. Rotate the flux tank by 90Â° on the dip plate and lift the dip plate out of the LDU together with the flux tank.

  8. Press 3. Remove park plate and drip tray: .

  9. Remove the park plate and then the drip tray .

The component parts can now be cleaned.
