LDU 2: Checking and adjusting the cavity on dipping plates that allow automatic setting of the cavity depth

Prerequisite: The dipping plate and park plate must be fitted and the dipping plate must be clean.

Proceed as follows to adjust the cavity depth of the dipping plate:

  1. Mount the linear dipping unit as specified.

  2. If the LDU is dismantled, fully assemble it first.

  3. Switch to the view Manual operations > X-Table , select the linear dipping unit and press Details...

  4. Press Automatic cavity depth -> Adjust cavity of dipping plate....

  5. Make sure there is no dipping medium on the dipping plate.

  6. Select a cavity depth to be set.

  7. Press Start.

  8. Use a suitable measuring instrument to check that the required cavity depth has been set exactly.

  9. If this is not the case:
    First choose an increment.
    Then press or to move the cavity of the dipping plate up or down by this amount.

  10. Press Accept to save the setting as soon as the exact specified depth has been reached.

  11. Remove the measuring device and acknowledge the prompt by pressing OK.
