Assembling the linear dipping unit (LDU 2)

To fit the linear dipping unit (LDU 2), proceed as follows:

  1. Switch to the view Setup > X-Table > , select the dip module and press Settings...

  2. Switch to the Dipping area tab.

  3. Press Reference run.

  4. Switch to the Cleaning tab.

  5. Press 1. Fit drip tray and park plate.

  6. Fit the drip tray and the park plate in the correct locations.

  7. Press 2. Fit dip plate.

  8. Insert the dipping plate with the flux tank .

  9. Press 3. Fit flux tank.
    The dipping plate axis moves down.

  10. Push the flux tank hard up against the slightly raised park plate to allow the flux tank to engage.

  11. Press 4. Move flux tank to park position .

You can now check for planarity and then fill the flux tank.
