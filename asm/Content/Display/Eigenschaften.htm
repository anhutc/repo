Properties

The Parameters section shows the parameters selected under Properties.

Global properties

The properties of the component which are not defined for individual groups, e.g. name and body size.

Groups (total number of groups)

The properties that apply to all groups (only for standard components).

Group (number of the group)

Properties of the specified group.

Corners (number of corners)

Corner

Properties of the specified corner.
Depending on the setting, the relevant corner points or the length, radius and angle are described.

Centering pins (number of centering pins or centering pin groups)

Properties which apply to all centering pins: Lead group = centering pin.

Centering pin

Properties of the centering pin group.

 

 

 

Hint:

Click this button and then click on the group, centering pins or corner in the camera image whose properties you wish to view.

The relevant properties are output as a list and the selected group, centering pin or corner is highlighted in color in the camera image.

If you click in the center of the camera image, the global properties are shown.
