Problems when measuring shield components

If there are problems measuring a shield component, the following checks should be carried out:
  1. Is the geometry described correctly, i.e. is the position of each corner correct?
  2. Are the sides of each corner of a similar length?
    Corners with sides of different lengths are more difficult to measure.
  3. Is the illumination set correctly?
    Double edges, oscillations etc. can possibly be suppressed or at least reduced by adjusting the illumination.
  4. Can the behavior be improved by changing the algorithm parameters?
