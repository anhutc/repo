Top surface features

In the case of LED components, the active regions can be described as top surface features: circles, rectangles and corners are possible.

The objective is to localize the active regions (position and angle) with reference to the component.

Parameter

Description

Global properties

Name

Name of the component shape.

Positional tolerance X/Y [mm]

Maximum permitted deviation of the position of the top surface feature in X or Y.*

Radial positional tolerance [mm]

Maximum permitted overall deviation of the orientation of the top surface feature.*

Maximum permitted rotational deviation of the top surface feature with reference to the component.

Special measurement options

Use angle correction

Yes

The component is rotated by the angle measured for the top surface feature.

No

The component is not rotated by the angle measured for the top surface feature. The angular positional tolerance that has been set is not checked.

Orientation

The position of the component is determined on the basis of:

Top / Bottom / Right / Left

of the corresponding outer edge of the active region relative to the coordinate system of the component shape in SIPLACE Pro.

Vertex centroid

of the vertex centroid of the detected rectangle.

Area centroid

of the area centroid of the rectangle.

Intersection of diagonals

of the intersection of the diagonals of the rectangle.

Features (To ensure accuracy, enter the nominal position of the features as specified in the data sheet during the teaching process.)

Describing circles

Diameter

Diameter of drilled hole or external contour.

Feature is light

Yes

This setting is used to describe a component with a rounded external contour.

No

Description of a drilled hole in a component.

Description of rectangular structures

X/Y

Coordinates of the center of the rectangle.

Angle

Angle of rotation of the rectangle with reference to the component.

Length / Width

External dimensions of the rectangular structure.

Maximum tolerance for length and width: 20 % of the corresponding nominal value.

Distance to corner

When detecting a rectangular structure, the corners with the edge lengths specified here are not taken into account.

Feature is light

 

 

Yes

Description of a rectangular area that appears bright compared with the body of the component or the complete component (contour measurement).

No

Description of a rectangular punched hole or of a rectangular dark area.
