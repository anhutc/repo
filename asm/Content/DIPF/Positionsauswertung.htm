Evaluation of position

To improve placement accuracy with components with nonstandard shapes (odd shape components - OSC), it may make sense if not all connection groups or features are used to determine the position of the component.

The "Evaluation of position" setting for components of the type "Nonstandard" or "Connector" now allows you to define whether the description of a connection group or feature is to be used to determine the overall position of the component or individual aspects of the component position (position in X, position in Y or angle of rotation).

In order to allow the position of the component to be identified, determination of the X position, the Y position and the angle must have been enabled for at least one connection group or feature.

The following settings are possible:

X, Y and angle

Default evaluation:
The position of the component in X and Y and the angle are determined.

Do not use

The group is not used for determining the position or angle of rotation of the component.

Angle

Determine the angle of rotation.

X and Y

Determine the position of the component in X and Y.

X

Determine the position of the component in X.

Y

Determine the position of the component in Y.

X and angle

Determine the position of the component in X and the angle of rotation.

Y and angle

Determine the position of the component in Y and the angle of rotation.

 

Note:

The "Evaluation of position" options are only shown if "Odd shape component" is enabled for the component shape in the programming system.
