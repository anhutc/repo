Description of corners

A corner is defined by two lines which are in turn defined by 2 points.

When defining corners, the sequence of the points is important: Viewed from Point 1, the body of the component should lie to the left of the lines.

 

The angle between these two lines should lie in the range 45째 through 135째 or 225째 through 315째 in order to make measurement possible.
