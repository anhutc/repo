Rectangular structures

With some components, it is possible to measure rectangular structures at any orientation.

Restrictions:

 

-

Rectangular features can only be described for component shapes of the type NONSTANDARD.

 

-

In the event of multiple measurements, the entire rectangle (taking account of the tolerances) must fit into approximately 2/3 of the field of view of the camera at every measurement angle.
