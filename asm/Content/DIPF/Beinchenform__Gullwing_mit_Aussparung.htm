Gullwing or wraparound with notch

The width of the notch must be specified with these leads. If the value 0.0 is entered for the notch, the lead is treated as a normal Gullwing or wraparound.

For leads with notches the filter points are set at the corners of the leads so that the filter results are not influenced by the notch.

 

 

The following applies for the width of the notch:

Width of notch <= 0.8 * width of lead
