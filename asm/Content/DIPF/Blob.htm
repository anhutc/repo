Lead shape: Blob

Irregular connecting surfaces with no assignable geometry. These are defined by a surrounding rectangle.

The center of gravity of the bright (type 90) or dark (type 91) area corresponds to the position of the feature.

 

 

 

Lead length and lead width

Extent of the surrounding rectangle in X and Y.

Offset X or Y

The center of gravity of the blob area in X and Y.

 

Caution:

Blob features only return a position, with no information about orientation. Depending on the reproducibility of the material properties and illumination, it is possible for inaccuracies to arise when determining the position.

 

Typical components: Non-standard
