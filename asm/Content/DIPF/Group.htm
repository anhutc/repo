Groups

Add group

The group data of the group defined before the currently selected group is displayed and the group is highlighted.

Remove group

The currently selected group is deleted and the one defined before this group is selected.

Change group

You can add or delete missing connections to/from the selected connection group:

Select missing leads (connections)

In the representation on the left, move the cursor over the leads/balls that are not present on the component. The corresponding connections of the model are marked with x. Connections marked in this way are not taken into account when measuring.

Delete missing leads (connections)

In the representation on the left, move the cursor over the connections. All of those marked x and all connections not currently recorded are now represented as connections to be measured.

Cancel

The changes made are rejected.
