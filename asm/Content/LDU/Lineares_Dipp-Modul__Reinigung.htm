Linear dipping unit: Cleaning

To clean the linear dipping unit (LDU):

  1. Switch to the "Production functions" menu.

  2. Choose Clean system and confirm your choice by clicking OK.
    The Y axis moves to the adjustment position.

  3. Remove the flux container and confirm with OK.
    The Y axis moves to the wait position.
    The Z axis moves to the dipping position.

  4. Remove the dipping plate and confirm with OK.
    The Z axis moves to the zero position.
    The Y axis moves to the adjustment position.

  5. Remove the park plate.

  6. Clean the components.

  7. Replace the park plate and confirm with OK.
    The Y axis moves to the wait position.
    The Z axis moves to the dipping position.

  8. Fit the dipping plate and confirm with OK.
    The Z axis moves to the zero position.
    The Y axis moves to the adjustment position.

  9. Fit the flux container and confirm with OK.
    The Y axis moves to the wait position.

  10. Initialize the system.
