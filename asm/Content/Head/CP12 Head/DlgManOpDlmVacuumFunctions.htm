Head > Vacuum

Common functionality

Actions

Measure pressure

Measures the activated forced air at the nozzle in the pickup position and in the holding circuit and shows the values.

Vacuum on

Switches on the vacuum at the nozzle in the pickup position.

Pressure on

Switches on set pressure at the nozzle in the pickup position.

Holding circuit [mbar]

Pressure measured in the holding circuit.

Placement circuit [mbar]

Measured pressure at the nozzle in the pickup position.

Advanced actions

Measure pressure (open/closed)

Measures the nozzle pressure in the pickup position with the nozzle open or closed. The measured values are displayed.

Placement circuit (open) [mbar]

Measured pressure at the open nozzle in the pickup position.

Placement circuit (closed) [mbar]

Measured pressure at the closed nozzle in the pickup position.
