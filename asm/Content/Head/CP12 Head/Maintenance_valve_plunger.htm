Perform valve plunger maintenance (C&P12 head)

Perform maintenance on the valve plunger as follows:

  1. Press the STOP button.

  2. Switch to the manual operations: .

  3. Select the appropriate head.

  4. Click Perform maintenance -> Valve plunger and select the appropriate segment.
    The head is moved to an easily reached position above the nozzle reject bin and the star axis rotates the head to the valve maintenance position.

  5. Open the protective cover.

  6. Check whether the valve plunger number corresponds to the selected segment number.
    If this is not the case, rotate the star axis manually.

  7. Remove the nozzle reject bin.

  8. Remove the valve plunger, clean it and replace it.

  9. If maintenance is to be performed on several valves rotate the star axis manually to the relevant maintenance position and repeat the operation for all the valves that are to be maintained.

  10. Replace the nozzle reject bin.

  11. Close the protective cover.

  12. Perform a head reference run:
    Click Reference head axis followed by Nozzles reference run.

  13. Press the START button.
