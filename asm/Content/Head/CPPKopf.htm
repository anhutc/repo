Collect&pick/place head:

This is a revolver head with 6 or 12 segments that can operate as a collect & place and as a pick & place head.

 

Collect & place:

Components up to a size of 27 mm x 27 mm can be picked up in succession by the individual segments and then placed in succession.

Pick & place:

Components larger than 30 mm x 30 mm must be measured with an external camera.

 

It is also possible to combine these modes. For example, 11 components can be picked up in succession in collect & place mode. When this has been done, the 12th component is picked up, measured with the external camera and placed. After this has been done, the remaining components can be placed in succession.
