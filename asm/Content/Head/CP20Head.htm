C&P20 head

20-segment collect&place head.

1. Compressed air connection for the 20 Venturi nozzles of the holding circuit

2. Star motor

3. Pressure regulation valve for the pickup and placement circuit

4. Component sensor

5. DP drives (20x)

6. Star with 20 nozzles

7. Component camera

8. "Holding circuit vacuum sensor" board

9. Intermediate distributor board

 

This is a revolver head with 20 segments that works on the collect & place principle.

 

Collect & place:

20 components can be picked up in sequence by the individual segments and then placed in sequence.
