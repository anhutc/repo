Manual operations > Head > Vacuum

Specialized

Functions

Adjust zero pressure point

Carries out zero calibration of the pressure regulator. The pressure regulator is set to the ambient pressure.

Procedure: Compressed air is switched off at the placement head. After 10 seconds, the ambient pressure is measured and the compressed air is then switched on again. The dialog box shows the results of the zero calibration.

Self-test air pressure

Carries out the pressure regulator selftest. The test results are then displayed in the dialog box.

Valve terminals (operations apply to checked segments)

Switch on

The connection to the nozzle tips is opened, thus creating the vacuum at the nozzle tips in the holding circuit.

Switch off

The connection to the nozzle tips is closed, thus removing the vacuum at the nozzle tips in the holding circuit.
Caution: If components are still on the nozzles in the holding circuit, they will be lost!

Segment

Number of the segment.

Status

Shows the status of the segment (On / Off).
