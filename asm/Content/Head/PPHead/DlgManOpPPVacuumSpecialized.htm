Manual operations > P&P head > Vacuum

Specialized

Adjust zero pressure point

Carries out zero calibration of the pressure regulator. The pressure regulator is set to the ambient pressure.

Procedure: Compressed air is switched off at the placement head. After 10 seconds, the ambient pressure is measured and the compressed air is then switched on again. The Results tab shows the results of the zero calibration.

Self-test air pressure

Carries out the pressure regulator selftest. The test results are then displayed in the dialog box.

Calibrate vacuum with closed nozzle

Places the nozzle on the conveyor edge so that the vacuum surface is closed and measures the vacuum value.
A special, type 518 nozzle is required for this function.

The value measured here is entered in the table Manual operations > P&P head > Vacuum values under "Closed (calibrated)".
