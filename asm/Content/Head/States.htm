Possible states

Not used

As a result of problems with the process, this segment is not to be used during the placement process.
- The nozzle is dirty/worn and there is no replacement nozzle available
- The filter disk is missing
- High error quota for a segment
- Hoses are leaking
disabled by user

The segment has been disabled by the operator:
- The segment is faulty
- The DP axis does not move freely
- The segment should be checked the next time regular maintenance is performed
disabled by software

Too many positioning errors have been reported for the segment.
The segment has become too hot and has been disabled automatically.
disabled: valve terminal The valve cannot be opened.
Deactivated: Nozzle is soiled The nozzle is dirty and there is no replacement nozzle available.
Deactivated: Nozzle is faulty The nozzle is damaged and can no longer be used for placement. No spare nozzle is available.
