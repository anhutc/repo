Conveyor lane width

Increase

The conveyor lane is widened by 0.1 mm by pressing the button.

Decrease

The changes made here only apply for teaching. They are not saved for production.

Board handling

Drive board into machine

Positions the board in the placement section and checks whether board position recognition can be performed. This is possible if at least two fiducials have been completely described.

Move board to output section

Moves the board to the output section, where it can be removed.

Move board out of machine

Moves the board into the downstream machine.
