Teaching a cluster

In order to teach a cluster, it is first necessary to determine the exact position of the reference panel and then to specify the row/column offsets.

Note: This is only possible if the cluster panels do not have any panel fiducials.

Choose the reference panel (at the bottom left in the example) and move the cluster to position it centrally in the camera image:

  

Select the panel at the top right and specify the row/column offsets:

Use the rotary controls to move the panel until the model completely overlays the camera image.

  

Then check whether all the panels are positioned correctly.
