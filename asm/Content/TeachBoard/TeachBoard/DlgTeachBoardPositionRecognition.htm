Board position recognition

If automatic board position recognition is not possible, you must align the board manual:

Board position recognition is not possible, because there are not at least two fully described fiducials available or selected for teaching. The exact position of the board must therefore be measured on the machine and stored. To do this, the rotation of the board must first be specified. To do this, select two points joined by a line parallel to either the longitudinal or lateral edge of the board.
If appropriate fiducials that share the same X or Y coordinate are available, these are displayed.

Hint: Choose two features on the board (e.g. fiducials or long conductor tracks or other lines) that share the same X or Y coordinate, i.e. that are parallel with the X or Y axis and that are as far apart as possible.

Top view: The board for rough positioning of the camera.

Mauve or light blue The camera can be positioned roughly by moving these regions. The portion of the camera image marked in mauve (bottom left) or light blue (bottom right) corresponds to the selected region.
Rectangle in these regions The small rectangle corresponds to the portion of the camera image shown in the bottom picture.

Bottom: The corresponding camera image

Coordinate system X and Y axes.
The two coordinate systems must be aligned in such a way that they form an axis in X or Y.
Hint: Do not align the coordinate system on the basis of the center of the feature, but rather on the basis of an outer boundary in order to enhance precision.

You can move the images either by moving the displayed camera image on the board or by pressing the Vision image or using the rotary controls. For the purposes of fine positioning, you should use the rotary controls and increase the adjustment sensitivity.

Caution: Make sure that you position the board accurately. Inaccurate positioning will have a negative impact on placement performance.

Settings: Pseudocolors / high sensitivity / crosshair
