Buttons for the camera image

 You can change the display with the following buttons:

Zooms and positions the camera image on the selected element.

Larger: Magnifies the camera image around the center of the image.

1:1 representation of the camera image.

Smaller: Reduces the camera image.
Moves the camera image in the direction indicated.

Meaning of the pointer icons:

Moves the panel model (all panels in the case of cluster panels).
Moves the camera image.
