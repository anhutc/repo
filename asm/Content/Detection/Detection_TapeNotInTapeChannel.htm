Check for protruding empty tape

The aim of this recognition step is to generate an error message in the event that tapes are not inserted properly in the waste tape chute. This is intended to prevent the placement head from being damaged by protruding empty tapes.

Example:

The tapes from tracks 7 and 9 are not inserted properly in the waste tape chute and trigger an error message.

Restriction: Inspection can only be carried out on machines on which the track scale has fiducial structures (circular fiducials) printed on it.

Tracks with white markings on the scale:

2 black circular fiducials

Tracks with black markings on the scale:

2 white circular fiducials

As soon as a feeder is attached, the check for protruding empty tapes is enabled and the corresponding fiducials are measured. If a tape is not inserted properly in the waste tape chute, the fiducials are hidden and cannot be measured.
