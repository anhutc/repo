Illumination by the component camera

The components are measured with LEDs arranged in a ring or as a flat surface at different angles to the component surface. This means that the components are illuminated from different angles.

Those LEDs arranged at a particular angle to the component surface are grouped as an illumination plane. The illumination intensity (illumination value) can be specified separately for each illumination plane.
