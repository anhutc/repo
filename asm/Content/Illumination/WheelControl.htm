Rotary control for setting the brightness value for illumination (intensity)

  • Values between 0 and 255 can be set.
  • Rotating the control increases or decreases the brightness value in increments of 5.
  • Pressing the left or right side of the rotary control reduces or increases the brightness value by 1.

 

Exception:

Camera 48 and 49:

  • With these cameras, it is possible to set percentages between 0 % and 100 % (green range) or up to 500 % (red range).
  • Rotating the control increases or decreases the value in increments of 2 %.
  • Pressing the left or right side of the rotary control reduces or increases the intensity value by 2 %.
  • Excess gain: If the component appears too dark, it is also possible to choose illumination values greater than 100 %.
    Caution: This can increase the time required for image capture.
