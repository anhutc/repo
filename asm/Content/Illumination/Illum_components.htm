Recommendation for illuminating components

 

CoS

What is measured?

Illumination

Chip: wraparound leads

(resistors, capacitors, tantalum components)

Lead areas

Outer dimensions (the lead surfaces are often rounded at the outer edges).

• steep illumination

• add some medium illumination levels between 30° and 60°

Melfs: Cylindrical components

(diodes, resistors)

Position recognition using leads at the caps.

• Even intensity from steep through flat

Gullwing components

(QFPs, SOTs, SSOPs, D-Packs)

Contact area (outer area of the leads with which the component makes contact)

 

• steep illumination

• Add some medium illumination levels:

Contact area of the leads is often not exactly parallel with the object level of the camera

 

J-leads

(PLCCs, SOJs)

Detection of the lead center.

• steep and medium illumination levels with approximately the same intensity

Balls

 

(BGAs, flip chips)

The balls are represented as rings.

• flat illumination only

CCGAs

Cylindrical pins with flat ends.

Cylindrical pins with flat ends.

• steep illumination,

• Possibly add some medium illumination levels

Shields

Measurement using the outer edges at the corners.

Set illumination so that the outer edges can be clearly seen.
