Background illumination

Viewed from the camera, the illumination is located "behind" the components. As a result, only outlines are detected, not the brightness of the component surface.

 

This setting is used in the SIPLACE BulkFeeder X for the detection of:

  • Component position
  • Component size
  • Tilted components (components that are lying on their side)
  • Component clusters (components that are not distanced from one another)
