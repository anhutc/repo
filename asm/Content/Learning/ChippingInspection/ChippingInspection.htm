Chipping inspection (component edge inspection)

Chipping inspection is intended to detect whether a component (die) shows signs of splintering or chipping on the cut edges that are to be flagged as defects:

 

Example:

Chipping is visible at the bottom edge of the component, which appears light.

 

You can specify whether chipping inspection is to be carried out at the programming system and directly in the component shape data (under Special measurement options).
