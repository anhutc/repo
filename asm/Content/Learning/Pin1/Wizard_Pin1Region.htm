Pin1 detection: Regions of interest

Select Pin1 region of interest:

Capture the area in which the Pin1 mark is located.

Symmetrically arranged comparison areas are then determined automatically.

Number of regions of interest:

2

One centrosymmetric comparison area is defined.
This setting is used for tantalum or LED component types, for instance.

4

Three comparison areas are defined, each of which is centrosymmetric and axisymmetric with the Pin1 region.

This setting is used for BGA, CLCC, QFN and special components, for instance.

 

Moving the region of interest for the pattern


The region of interest is moved by one increment to the left, right, up or down.

Select the point in the image that you wish to define as the center of the region of interest.

Size of the regions of interest: Width and height

The width or height is increased or decreased by 1 increment.

Step size

The value by which the specified parameter is moved or resized by clicking one of the buttons shown above.

The increment is increased or decreased.

You can also enter any of these values directly from the keyboard by clicking the corresponding field and entering the value you require.
