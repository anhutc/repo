Pattern matching

With this measurement method, the position is determined on the basis of the inner structures of components.

This is necessary with components that have no connections (bare dies) if the outer cut edges do not meet the extremely high precision requirements.

 

Start the pattern matching wizard as follows:

  1. Switch to the view "List of measurement options" .
  2. Choose the measurement option Pattern matching.
  3. Click Edit parameters.

The wizard is opened showing the Set illumination view.
