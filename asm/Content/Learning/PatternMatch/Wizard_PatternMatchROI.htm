Pattern matching: Specify size and position of pattern

Here you can specify the region of the camera image in which the pattern is to be learned.

Pale blue rectangle

Region of interest for the pattern.

 

Hint: The size of the region of interest should be defined in such a way that the outer edges lie outside this region.

If this is not done, the outer edges will become part of the model, which is not desirable because the cut edges may not be accurate.

 

Calculate automatically

The region of interest is calculated and selected on the basis of the outer contour of the component.

 

Moving the region of interest for the pattern


The region of interest is moved by one increment to the left, right, up or down.

Select the point in the image that you wish to define as the center of the pattern.

Rotating the pattern rectangle

The coordinate system of the region of interest is rotated one increment to the left or right.

Width and height of pattern

The width or height is increased or decreased by 1 increment.

Step size

The value by which the specified parameter is moved, rotated or resized by clicking one of the buttons shown above.

The increment is increased or decreased.

You can also enter any of these values directly from the keyboard by clicking the corresponding field and entering the value you require.
