Pattern matching: Exclusion areas

The exclusion areas defined here are ignored during teaching and evaluation.

Recommendation:

Structures that are not present on all components or that are not clearly visible on all components should not be evaluated.

 

Yellow rectangle

Defined region of interest.

Pale blue rectangle

Currently selected area that is not to be evaluated (exclusion area).

Green rectangle

Exclusion area that has already been defined.

 

Add area

Manually define an area that is not to be evaluated.

  1. Click Add area.
  2. A cross is shown in the image:
    Draw the exclusion area by clicking in the window and then moving your finger over the touchscreen or moving the mouse pointer over the image while holding the left mouse button down (the position at which you pressed the left mouse button indicates the top left corner and the position at which you released the left mouse button indicates the bottom right corner).
    The areas are created parallel to the region of interest.

Remove area

The currently selected area (shown in pale blue in the camera image and highlighted in color in the list) is deleted and will not be evaluated.

Position X/Y

Center of the exclusion area.

Width X / Length Y

Dimensions of the exclusion area.
