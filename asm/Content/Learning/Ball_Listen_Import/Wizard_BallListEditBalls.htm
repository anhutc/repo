Importing a ball list: Edit list

This view allows you to check whether the ball list matches the component shape shown in the camera image and to supplement any information that may be missing from the ball list.

By changing the representation within the ROI, the user can check whether the edited position list is adequate as a component shape description.

Representation

In this view, you can position the described model in the camera image within the presentation tolerance.

The aim is to position the model as precisely as possible over the image in the camera view.

You can specify the position of the model by then clicking in the camera image:

First click the button and then click on the position in the camera image at which the center of the model should be located.

Arrow keys:

Each time you click on the corresponding button, the model is moved up, down, to the left or to the right by the increment that has been set.

Each time you click on the corresponding button, the model is rotated clockwise or anticlockwise by the angle increment that has been set.

Each time you click the button, the increment or angle increment is increased or decreased.

Hint:

First position the model roughly by clicking the button and then clicking the required position in the image.

Set the exact position using the arrow keys.
