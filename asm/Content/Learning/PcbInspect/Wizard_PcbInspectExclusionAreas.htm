Inspection area: Exclusion areas for "Inspection before placement"

Here you can define individual areas within the inspection area in which inspection is not to be carried out.

The exclusion areas are not evaluated

  • No height is calculated for these areas. They are hidden in the height maps for the model and the current board.

  • The presence of any components that have already been learned for this area is not checked.

Green

Component body: A model based on the component shape description is shown.

Orange (struck through)

Teaching areas.

Yellow (struck through)

Exclusion areas.

Add area

Manually define an area that is not to be evaluated.

  1. Click Add area.

  2. A cross is shown in the image:
    Draw the exclusion area by clicking in the window and then moving your finger over the touchscreen or moving the mouse pointer over the image while holding the left mouse button down (the position at which you pressed the left mouse button indicates the top left corner and the position at which you released the left mouse button indicates the bottom right corner).

Any number of exclusion areas can be created. This does not mean that the model has to be learned again.

Remove area

The currently selected area (shown in pale blue in the camera image and highlighted in color in the list) is deleted and will not be evaluated.
