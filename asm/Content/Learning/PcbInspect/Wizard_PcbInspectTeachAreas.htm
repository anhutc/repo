Inspection area: Teaching areas

Here, you can define specific areas in which solder deposits (SPI) or components that have already been placed (BPI) are to be learned during the teaching process.

The solder deposits or components that have been learned during teaching are checked during inspection.

The contents of the teaching areas are preset on the basis of the component shape description.

 

Tips on the inspection of solder deposits:

•

Specify a teaching area for each lead group.

•

Define the teaching areas in such a way that all solder deposits are fully contained in a teaching area and there is an additional border around the solder deposits.

Tips on the specifications regarding inspection before or after placement:

•

Where possible, define the teaching areas in such a way that they do not contain any solder deposits.

•

Define the teaching areas in such a way that all components are fully contained in a teaching area and there is an additional border around the components.

•

Where possible, the components in one teaching area should all be of a similar height. If this is not the case, you should create multiple areas.

•

If individual components are obscured by higher components in the vicinity, set the parameter Single component (identical size) to Yes and define separate teaching areas for each of these obscured components. These should be exactly the same size as the corresponding component.

Add area

Manually define an area that is to be learned:

  1. Click Add area.

  2. A cross is shown in the image:
    Draw the teaching area by clicking in the window and then moving your finger over the touchscreen or moving the mouse pointer over the image while holding the left mouse button down (the position at which you pressed the left mouse button indicates the top left corner and the position at which you released the left mouse button indicates the bottom right corner).

Remove area

The currently selected area (shown in pale blue in the camera image and highlighted in color in the list) is deleted and will not be evaluated.
