43897 The reference point not defined or moved

The conveyor rails do not move freely or there is an obstacle in the travel path.

Switch to the view Manual operations > Subsystems > Shuttle Extension and check whether the conveyor rails can be moved.

The motors were not calibrated correctly.

Switch to the view Service > Shuttle configuration > Shuttle initiation and perform calibration for the shuttle motor and the width adjustment motor.

 

Hardware problems (belt, motor, ...).

Check all hardware components and recalibrate the motors after any mechanical work has been carried out.
