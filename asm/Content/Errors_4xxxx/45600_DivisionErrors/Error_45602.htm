45602 Tape duct unloaded

The tape has been moved back and is engaged with the rear pinwheel. It must not be inserted again and must be removed and cut off.

  • Select the tape duct:

    Tape duct 1: Press the button briefly once.

    Tape duct 2: Press the button briefly twice.

  • Press the Back button on the feeder briefly.

  • Remove the tape, cut it and then insert it again.
