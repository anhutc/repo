45606 Tape drive blocked

The tape reel for the tape duct indicated is jammed or twisted.

Make sure that the tape reel is not blocked and can be moved easily.

Tape is jammed or twisted.

Check the tape feeder.

If the error message is only issued for one of the rear pinwheels of the specified tape duct:

Remove the tape, cut it and insert it as specified.

If the drive of the front pinwheel is also affected:

Components are jamming the front pinwheel.

Check the pinwheel and remove any components that are blocking it.

Then press the Forward button on the feeder to check the drive.

 

If problem still exists:

Remove both tapes, check them, cut the tapes and insert them again.
