43114 Calibrated up position of vacuum tooling is out of range

 

Make sure that the vacuum tooling type has been installed correctly and is not dirty.

 

Check the defined touchdown positions:

Switch to the view Service > Teach machine positions . For the relevant lane, select all vacuum tooling touchdown positions in sequence and, for each, press Move to position. Make sure that the defined touchdown positions are not in a depression and change them if necessary.
