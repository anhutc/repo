40081 Nozzle had frequent errors

Frequent errors have been detected on the specified nozzle.

The nozzle is therefore scanned and the segment is temporarily deactivated.

 

Clean nozzle: An attempt is made to clean the nozzle with a pulse of forced air and then to take the segment into operation again.

If the nozzle cannot be cleaned and there is a spare nozzle in the nozzle changer:

Return nozzle and pick up new nozzle: The nozzle is returned to the nozzle changer and replaced with another from the nozzle changer.

If there are no more nozzles of the same type in the nozzle changer and the nozzle can therefore not be replaced:

Return nozzle to nozzle changer: The nozzle is returned to the nozzle changer.

Then clean the nozzle by hand or replace it. Switch to the view Setup > Nozzle changer magazine functions for the corresponding head, select the garage and choose Nozzle in garage is clean from the Actions (Confirm) item of the drop-down menu.

Do not use segment: If it is not possible to clean or replace the nozzle, the segment must be deactivated to allow placement to continue with the remaining segments.

Caution: Deactivation of the segment reduces the placement performance!

You can also clean the nozzle on the head by hand. Then press Nozzle cleaned by hand.
