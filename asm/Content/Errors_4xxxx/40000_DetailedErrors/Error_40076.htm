40076 Free garages are needed

More unoccupied garages are needed in order to take the support pins from the lifting table and replace them in the garages.

  1. Remove some support pins from the magazines at the specified locations by hand or set up a Smart Pin Magazine T5 with empty garages.

  2. When there are enough empty garages, confirm this by pressing Garages are free.
