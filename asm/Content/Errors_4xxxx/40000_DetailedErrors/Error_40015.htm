40015 Support pin is missing in garage

A support pin is expected at the garage marked in red, but the garage is empty.

The missing support pin can be replaced:

Place a support pin in the marked garage by hand and confirm that you have done so: Support pin inserted.

The missing support pin has been removed from the garage by hand:

Confirm that this has been done: Support pin removed.

The support pin is then no longer expected in the garage.
