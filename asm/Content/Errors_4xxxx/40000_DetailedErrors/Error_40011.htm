40011 Incompatible feeder eSW version detected

A more recent version of the embedded software is available for at least one X-Feeder.

Update feeders: Wait at least 5 seconds after mounting the last X-Feeder before carrying out the action.

Caution: Feeders must not be removed or mounted during the update process.

Note: Once the download has been completed successfully, production is started automatically.
