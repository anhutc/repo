40072 Board has been locked before entering the input section

The board is being prevented from moving in by an external unit (e.g. the Board Gate Keeper).

Move board into machine and cancel board: The board is moved into the machine and transported through without being assembled. It must be removed at the end of the placement line.

Unlock board and place board: The lock imposed by the external system (e.g. Board Gate Keeper) is removed and the board is assembled.
