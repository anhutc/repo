40018 Cannot place support pin

There is already a support pin at the required position.

Remove the support pin from the pin picker by hand and confirm that this has been done: Support pin removed.

 

The support pin cannot be put down correctly due to obstructions on the lifting table, in the magazine or on the underside of the support pin.

Clean the support pin and the lifting table and ensure that the garage is empty. Confirm that this has been done: Cleaning actions carried out.
