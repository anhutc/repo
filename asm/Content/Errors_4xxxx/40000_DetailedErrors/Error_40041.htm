40041 Board to be produced is larger than mapped area

A job was specified with a board that is larger than the mapping plate that was used to perform mapping.

The specified job is canceled. Mapping must be carried out again.

Service engineer: Carry out mapping using the larger mapping plate (SX mapping plate, Version 5):

Switch to the view Service > Automatic calibration , select the calibration steps Board mapping and Head mapping and perform the mapping.

Confirm that this has been done: Mapping carried out.

 

Ignore mapping error: The error is ignored and the job is not canceled.

Caution: Placement accuracy can decrease if you continue working with inappropriate mapping data.
