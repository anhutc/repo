40009 Action at nozzle changer failed, user intervention required

The sleeve was withdrawn from the segment while returning the nozzle to the nozzle changer

Caution: The placement head and gantry must not be moved during this operation.

Check the sleeve on the specified segment and removed the nozzle manually if necessary.

Carefully push the sleeve into the head again.

Rotate the star axis to the park position so that it cannot drop down

 

Then confirm that the sleeve is seated correctly: Actions done by hand.
