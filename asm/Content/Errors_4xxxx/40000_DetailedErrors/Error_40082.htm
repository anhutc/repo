40082 Fiducials on the track ruler cannot be measured

The fiducials are dirty or damaged.

Try to clean the track scale at the specified position and replace it if necessary.

The empty tape was not fed into the waste tape chute correctly and is covering the track scale on the specified track.

Feed the empty tape into the waste tape chute correctly.

 

Press Tape position checked when the track scale is clean and the tape has been fed into the waste tape chute correctly.
