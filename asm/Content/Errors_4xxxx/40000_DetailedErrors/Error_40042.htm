40042 CAUTION: Placement head might collide with nozzle changer

If a C&P20Plus head is fitted in place of a CPP head, the nozzle changer on the opposite location must be fitted 3.65 mm lower than normal.

Fit the nozzle changer at the lower position and confirm that this has been done: Confirm nozzle changer position.
