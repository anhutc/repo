40023 CAUTION: Do not move gantry! Segment is down

 

A number of different measures are necessary to rectify this error, depending on the head type.

Notify your service provider to carry out the necessary measures.

Then confirm that this has been done: Actions done by hand.

 

The component sensor is covered or faulty.

The segment is jammed in the nozzle changer (CPP or C&P20 head).

The segment does not move freely, the Z axis cannot be referenced.

The return cylinder is set too low (C&P20 head).

The segment is jammed in the nozzle changer (TWIN head or C&P12 head):

The segment cannot be referenced.

If none of the suggested solutions is successful, the machine must be shut down.
