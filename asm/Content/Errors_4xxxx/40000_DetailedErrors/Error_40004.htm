40004 Fiducial recognition failed

In the section marked in red, it was not possible to recognize a fiducial, or the gap to another fiducial is too small.

Example: Right-hand conveyor lane, placement section 2.

 

Correct fiducial position: Teach the fiducial again.

 

If it is not possible to measure the fiducial:

First open the drop-down menu and specify the position at which you want to remove the board. Then press Fiducial cannot be taught: cancel board.

 

If a barcode is being used as a fiducial:

Enter the fiducial barcode and press Barcode entered by hand.
