40033 Prerequisites for changing conveyor width must be fulfilled

Not all the prerequisites for adjusting the conveyor width have been met.

Make sure that the necessary activity has been carried out manually and confirm that this has been done.

Examples of conditions that have not been met:

There are still support pins on the lifting table.

Remove all support pins from the lifting table by hand before proceeding. Confirm that this has been done: All pins removed from lifting table.

The configuration of the fixed conveyor rails is not appropriate for the specified product.

Service engineer: Go to the specified side of the machine, fit the fixed conveyor rail as specified and confirm that this has been done: Fixed conveyor rail mounted as selected.

The configuration of the fixed conveyor rails is not appropriate for the specified product.

Service engineer: Convert the conveyor accordingly and confirm that this has been done: Position of fixed conveyor rail adjusted.

When converting from a dual conveyor to a single conveyor (setting: Dual as single), it is necessary to couple the two lifting tables of a placement area.

Service engineer: Couple the lifting tables mechanically and confirm that this has been done: Lifting tables coupled.

When converting from a single conveyor (setting: Dual as single) to a dual conveyor, it is necessary to decouple the two lifting tables of a placement area.

Service engineer: Decouple the lifting tables mechanically and confirm that this has been done: Lifting tables decoupled.
