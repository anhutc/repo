40049 Action at nozzle changer failed, user intervention required

A magazine has inadvertently been pulled from its mounting when changing a nozzle in the nozzle changer.

Caution: The placement head and gantry must not be moved during this operation.

Open the protective cover, remove the nozzle changer magazine carefully from the segment and replace it in the nozzle changer.

Check that the nozzle is seated properly and check that it can also be released from the segment.

 

Then confirm that the magazine has been removed from the segment by hand, that it is now located properly in the nozzle changer and that the nozzle is engaged on the segment: Actions done by hand.
