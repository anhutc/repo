40077 Support pins are needed

More support pins are required than are currently available.

Example:

Location x The number of support pins specified here are required at location x. 3
Location x or y The number of support pins specified here can be provided either from location x or from location y. 4
Location y The number of support pins specified here are required at location y. 2
Needed pins in total 9

  1. Provide the required support pins either in free garages or using additional Smart Pin Magazines.

  2. Once sufficient support pins are available, confirm this by pressing Support pins inserted.
