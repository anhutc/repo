Teaching a placement position

Placement positions can be checked and if necessary corrected before components are placed.

It is often the case that centering pins or springs are not specified or not specified precisely enough in the component shape description. To ensure that the centering pins meet the feedthroughs on the board exactly, it may be necessary to correct individual placement positions during production.

The placement position to be taught can be specified in SIPLACE Pro and at the station.

After the teaching process, the changed placement position must be saved in the board description in SIPLACE Pro.

Proceed as follows to specify what placement position is to be taught before placement at the station:

  1. Go to the station at which the placement position is to be placed, switch to the Production view and stop the current placement process: .

  2. At this station, switch to the view Board > Placement position list for a lane .

  3. Select the relevant lane and then select the entry you require from the list (multiple selection is also possible).

  4. Press the Teaching required button.

  5. Switch back to the Production view and press the Start button .

 

Proceed as follows to correct the placement position manually:

Production is stopped and the Placement position list view is displayed for one lane . The placement position to be placed next is selected.

  1. Press View placement position to open the Placement position adjustment view.

  2. Use the buttons to position the component in such a way that the pins meet the feedthroughs on the board as accurately as possible.

  3. Save the corrected values in SIPLACE Pro by pressing Accept.

  4. Switch back to the Production view and press the Start button .

 

Caution:

Because the placement positions are subsequently not subjected to a feasibility test, collisions with other components or changed cycle times can occur.
