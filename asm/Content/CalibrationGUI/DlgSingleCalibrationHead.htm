Identify head parameter

Determines the star axis system parameters for the current head again as a result of wear or humidity. This insures that optimum values are used.

Head offset

Calibration of the head offset will be performed. This determines the offset between the board camera and segment 1 when the Z axis is extended (Z axis down).

Calibrating the head offset (method for the Twin Head)

Head cameras

The head camera(s) are calibrated. This determines the camera coefficients and the camera angle. The focus height is also determined for stationary cameras.

Calibrating the head camera (method)

Calibrating the head cameras (method for the Twin Head)

Segments

Determines the segment offsets of the selected segments.

Calibrating segments (method)

Calibrating segments (method for the Twin Head)

Calibrate head cameras and all segments

Calibrates the head camera(s) and all segments. Values are determined for the camera coefficient, the camera angle and the segment offsets.

Calibrating the head (method for the Twin Head)

Calibrate P&P comp.camera high resolution with glass plate

Determines the focal height of the component camera and adjusts the component camera and the board camera in such a way that the components can be measured from above and below at the same position.

Adjustment of zero pressure point

Calibrates the zero pressure point: Compensates for location-dependent discrepancies in the ambient air pressure.

 

Execute single calibration

The selected calibration step is started.

Segment

Segment number.

Lower X [µm] and Lower Y [µm]

The deviation from the default offset of the segment from the board camera when the Z axis is in the lower position, in X and Y respectively.

SST Upper X [µm] and Y [µm]

The deviation from the default offset of the center of rotation of the tip of the nozzle to the component camera.

If a text is shown in red, a value limit has been exceeded.

Apply

Saves the values determined during calibration in the machine data.

Close

Terminates single calibration without saving the data.
