Calibrating the reject position (method)

 

Notes:

Before the calibration process is carried out, the calibration data for the board camera, the component camera, the C&P20 head or CPP head, the head offset and the machine zero point must already have been calculated (see Calibrating the head).

To calibrate the height (Z position), a nozzle must be attached to the bottom segment.

  1. Perform a reference run of all head and gantry axes. To do this, open the "Manual operations > Entire machine" view and press the Overall reference run button.
  2. Open the "Service" view and press the Single calibration button.
  3. In the "Service > Single calibration" view, press the Calibrate nozzle changer button for the required nozzle changer. This opens the Calibrate nozzle changers dialog box.
  4. Press the Single selection button.
  5. Press the button or select the corresponding entry (RS stands for Reject Station) from the table.
  6. Press Start. Calibration of the reject position will now be performed. The board camera moves over the positioning fiducials of the reject station and measures their X and Y positions. The nozzle is used to determine the current Z position of the reject station and thus determines the specified height for dropping the nozzles in the reject station.
  7. Press Save to save the data determined during calibration in the machine data.
