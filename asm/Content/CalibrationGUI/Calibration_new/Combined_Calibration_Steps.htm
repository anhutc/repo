Combined calibration steps

Calibration steps that belong together are offered as combinations in order to obviate the need to call each calibration step separately.

These combinations contain the following individual steps (certain steps may not be present, depending on the machine type):

Main axes calibration:

- Y axis encoder alignment

- Travel range calibration

- Y axis mapping

- Axis zero correction

- System identification

- X axis parametrization

Calibration of fiducials, cameras and heads:

- Board camera calibration

- Board reference corner calibration

- Head and cameras

- Nozzle changer calibration

- Vacuum tooling calibration

Board and head mapping:

- Board mapping

- Head mapping

Pin picker calibration (only appears if a pin picker is present on the machine):

- Lifting table calibration

- Pin picker offset calibration

- Pin magazine calibration
