Gantry axis calibration

  1. Open the "Service > Automatic calibration" view .
  2. Select the calibration step Travel range and press Next.
  3. Select the gantry or gantries and press Start. The gantry axes are calibrated. This determines the minimum and maximum end positions of the X and Y axes and the corresponding travel paths.
  4. After calibration has been completed successfully, press Accept to save the data determined during calibration in the machine data.

Note:

During calibration, the gantries moved together in order to measure the gap between them.
