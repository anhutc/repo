Calibrate Twin Head (method)

 

  1. Make sure that the sleeves of both segments of the Twin Head are fitted with a nozzle of type 517.
  2. Put the calibration tool into the calibration tool pocket keeping the printed (matt) side facing downward.
  3. Perform a reference run of all head and gantry axes. To do this, open the "Manual operations > Entire machine" view and press the Overall reference run button.
  4. Open the "Service" view and press the Single calibration button.
  5. In the "Service > Single calibration" view, press the Calibrate head button for the required Twin Head. The dialog box Service > Single calibration > Gantry n > Head opens.
  6. Choose Head cameras and all segments from the drop-down menu and press Execute single calibration.
    Calibration of the entire head will now be performed. Values are determined for the camera coefficient, the camera angle and segment offsets for both segments.
  7. After calibration has been completed successfully, press Accept to save the values determined during calibration in the machine data.
