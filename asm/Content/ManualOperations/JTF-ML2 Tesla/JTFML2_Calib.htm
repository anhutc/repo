Teach transfer position: Setting the lifting axis of the Multiple JEDEC Tray Feeder

1.  Make sure that both cassettes are in the feeder and that the bottom level of the top cassette contains a tray (cassettes with 9 levels in the example):

2.  Switch to the view Manual operations > Location > X-Table > , select the feeder and press Details....

3.  Switch to Functions.

4.  Press Transfer position > Teach position.

5.  Press Start.
The lift is moved in such a way that the bottom waffle pack tray carrier of the top cassette is in the transfer position.

6.  Use the increment selection control and the and buttons to adjust the lift in such a way that the tray can be moved out of the cassette without any difference in height:

7.  Press Accept to save the new transfer position.
