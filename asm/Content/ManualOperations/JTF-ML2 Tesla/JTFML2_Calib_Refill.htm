Teach refill position: Setting the lifting axis of the Multiple JEDEC Tray Feeder to allow the cassettes to be refilled

To teach the refill position, the lifting axis must be adjusted so as to allow the cassettes to be removed from and inserted into the tower without force:

1.  Make sure that both cassettes are in the feeder. The cassettes must both have the same number of levels.

2.  Switch to the view Manual operations > Location > X-Table , select the JTF-ML2 feeder and press Details....

3.  Switch to Functions.

4.  Press Refill position > Teach position.

5.  Press Start.
The lift is moved in such a way that the top cassette is in the currently saved refill position and can be removed. The lift door is unlocked.

6.  Open the lift door, pull out the top cassette and push it back in again.

7.  If force is required to remove or insert the cassette, the refill position must be corrected:

  • Close the lift door.
  • Use the increment selection control and the and buttons to adjust the lift so that the top cassette can be removed from and inserted into the tower without any difference in height
  • Press Accept to save the new refill position.

8.  If necessary, repeat steps 5 and 6 until the cassette can be removed and replaced without force.
