Location > Tape cutter

Cut

Moves the tape cutter to the end position and back again twice and cuts off the residual material of the component tapes.

The time required for this is entered under "Time measurement".

Tape cutter status

The tape cutter has a horizontal cutter that cuts the tape by moving forwards, driven by two air cylinders mounted at the left and right ends. The cylinders pull it back again after it has cut the tape.
