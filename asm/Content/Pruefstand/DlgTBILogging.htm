Test bench inspection

The views of the test bench inspection are not supported in the current version of the Help system.

 

The aim of the test bench inspection is to check the condition of the placement heads in the machine and verify that they are functioning properly.

A number of different test and measurement procedures are available for testing the different types of heads (CPP, C&P20A, C&P20P and Twin Head (HCS II only)). These procedures can be employed individually or in their entirety.

When inspection is complete, the results are collated in a test report. This report can also be saved as a PDF file. The results make it possible to draw conclusions about the condition of the heads and, where necessary, take measures to ensure or increase the availability of the line from a technical perspective.
