Hotbar process

Thermodes: Heating elements for the hotbar process (thermode soldering)

There are currently 6 thermodes on the lifting table for the hotbar process.

As soon as a carrier has been clamped and the thermodes have reached the required temperature, the thermodes are moved up so that they make direct contact with the bottom of those placement positions on the board for which this method has been specified. The second component type is then placed on the placement positions. The two stacked components are joined by the heated adhesive.

Procedure:

-

The board or carrier moves into the placement section

-

The board is clamped

-

The thermodes heat up

-

The thermodes move to the top position and the wait time starts

-

The board is aligned

-

Wait until the required wait time has elapsed

-

Pick up and place the second component type.

-

Thermodes move to the bottom position

-

Release board clamping

If placement is interrupted, the thermodes must be moved to the bottom position.
