Conveyor (single, dual or quad lane conveyor)

The SIPLACE machines can be operated with single conveyors, dual conveyors or quad lane conveyors.

 

The following restrictions apply when a quad-lane conveyor is used:

•

The two lanes 2L and 2R and 1L and 1R each form a lane pair that cannot be operated independently.

•

When boards are loaded manually, the boards for a lane pair must be loaded almost simultaneously.
