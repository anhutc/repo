Recognition step: Glue dot x

Shows the component and the result of a glue dot measurement. The diameter determined for this glue dot is output below the representation.

Yellow frame

Target area in which the glue dots is expected.

Green outline

Detected edge of the glue dot.

Green cross

Center of the detected glue dot.

Red outline

The glue dot was detected, but:

• The detected glue dot extends beyond the target area.
• More than one glue dot was detected in the target area.
• The detected glue dot is either larger or smaller than the specified tolerance.

Red cross

Center of the glue dot which has been identified as incorrect.

 

Examples of errors:

The glue dot is too large: The measured diameter of the glue dot is larger than the specified upper limit (upper limit = diameter + diameter tolerance).

The glue dot is too small: The measured diameter of the glue dot is smaller than the specified lower limit (lower limit = diameter - diameter tolerance).
