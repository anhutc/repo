Recognition step: Dispenser nozzle

Shows the extent to which the dispenser nozzle is dirty.

The position and area of the dispenser nozzle duct are determined.

 

Yellow frame

Target area in which the dispenser nozzle duct is expected.

Green highlighting

The detected dispenser nozzle is within the target area.
The shape of this marking can give an indication of dirtiness.

Red highlighting

The dispenser nozzle is dirty or is not within the target area.
