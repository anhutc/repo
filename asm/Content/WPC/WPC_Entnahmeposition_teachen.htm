WPC: Teaching the removal position

 

  1. Press Teach remove position and then Next.
  2. Press Next again to start the reference run.
  3. Press the button for the cassette whose removal position  is to be taught and confirm your selection by clicking Next. The WPC is moved to the refill position.
  4. Insert a waffle pack tray carrier in the required level and confirm that you have done this by pressing Next. The WPC is moved to the removal position.
  5. If necessary, teach the removal position using the and buttons. (Every click of these buttons moves the feed axis one step of the selected step size in the corresponding direction.)
  6. Press Engage catch into WPTC to check the current position.
  7. Press Finish to store the current position as the removal position in the WPC's internal machine data.
