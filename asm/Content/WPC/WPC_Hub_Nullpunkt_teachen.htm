WPC: Teaching the zero position (lifting axis)

 

  1. Select the lifting axis .
  2. Press Teach zero position and then Next.
  3. Follow the instructions shown and press Next.
  4. Try to push the tray carrier into the tower by hand.
    If necessary, teach the height of the lifting axis using the and buttons so that the tray carrier can be moved into the tower without any resistance.
    (Every press of these buttons moves the lifting axis one step of the selected step size in the corresponding direction.)
  5. Press Finish to store the current position as the zero position for the lifting axis in the WPC's internal machine data.
