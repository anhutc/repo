Choose board options

Cancel board

Assembly of the selected board is not continued, irrespective of the current location of the board.

After assembly of the board has been canceled, the board will be highlighted in red in the display.

Here you can choose whether the board is to be removed from the output area (output section or output shuttle) of the current station or from the output area of the last station in the line. The board stops at the selected position.

Mark for inspection

Depending on the selection made under , the board stops in the output section of the current station or in the output area of the last station in the line, where it can be inspected.

Skip placement

In PA 1

Components are not placed on the selected board in processing area 1.
(This option can only be checked if the board is located in the input section.)

In PA 2

Components are not placed on the selected board in processing area 2.
(This option can only be checked if the board is located in the input section, placement section or intermediate section.)
