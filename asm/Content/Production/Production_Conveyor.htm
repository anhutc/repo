Representation of the conveyor in the Basic production view

Pressing a board opens the Board options view.

Pressing the button (red and white bar) below the representation of the conveyor opens a dialog box in which you can lock or unlock the corresponding lane for subsequent boards. If a conveyor lane is locked, no new boards are loaded.
