Configure carrier (C&P20 head)

1. Select carrier

Select the magazine carrier you wish to change.

indicates the magazine carrier you see first when you are standing on the relevant side of the machine.

2. Select carrier type

Select the appropriate type:

Magazine carrier with 6 magazines.
 

6x (12x 10XX)
6x (12x 20XX)
6x (9x 28XX)

Magazine carrier with 6 magazines, each of which can be loaded with 12 10-series nozzles, 12 20-series nozzles or 9 28-series nozzles.

6x (12x 10XX)

Magazine carrier with 6 magazines, each of which can be loaded with 12 10-series nozzles.

Magazine carrier not present (corresponds to an empty magazine carrier location).

Accept

The changes are saved.

Close

The dialog box is closed and any changes which have not been saved are discarded.
