Actions (Confirm)

Confirm nozzles in all magazines

Confirms manual change of all the nozzles of all magazines. It is subsequently assumed that the specified nozzles are in the magazines.

Confirm nozzles in magazine n

Confirms manual change of all the nozzles of the selected magazine. It is subsequently assumed that the specified nozzles are in the magazine.

Confirm nozzle in garage n

Confirms manual change of the nozzle in the selected garage. It is subsequently assumed that the specified nozzle is in the garage.

First select the appropriate entry from the list, (the nozzles to be changed are marked with ), change the nozzle as specified and then press the button.

Empty garage n

You should press this button when you have manually removed the nozzle from the garage.

It is subsequently assumed that the garage is empty.

Nozzle in garage x is clean

Nozzles in magazine x are clean

All nozzles in all magazines are clean

By your selection, you confirm that one nozzle has been cleaned or another, clean nozzle has been inserted in the garage(s).

This confirmation must also be done if one or more nozzles are replaced and replacement has been acknowledged, if the nozzles had previously been flagged as "dirty".

Actions (Scan)

Scanning nozzles in all magazines

Scan nozzles in magazine n

Scan nozzle in garage n

Depending on the selection you make here, the board camera is positioned over a single garage, over all the garages of a specific magazine in sequence or over all the garages of the nozzle changer and nozzle detection is started.

The system checks whether there is a nozzle in the garage and whether this nozzle has a nozzle ID. If a nozzle with a nozzle ID is detected, the nozzle type can be determined and entered under "Current" in the table.

Lock

Open

The magazines of the selected magazine carrier are opened to allow you to manually refill the magazine.

Close

The magazines of the selected magazine carrier are closed.

Shows the current status of the nozzle changer: open or locked.
