Cleaning a dirty nozzle

If a dirty nozzle is detected that cannot be cleaned with a pulse of compressed air and cannot be replaced, you should remove this nozzle from the nozzle changer and clean it manually:

1.  Switch to the view Setup > Nozzle changer .
The dirty nozzle is indicated by

2.  Remove the nozzle from the garage indicated.

3.  Clean the nozzle and replace it in the garage.

4.  From the Actions (confirm) drop-down menu, choose Nozzle in garage is clean.

 

If the nozzle cannot be cleaned, it must be replaced:

1.  Select the nozzle from the list or from the graphical display and from the Actions (confirm)drop-down list, choose Empty garage.

2.  Place a new nozzle of the same type in the garage and from the Actions (confirm) drop-down menu, choose Confirm nozzle in garage.
