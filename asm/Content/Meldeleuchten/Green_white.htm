Green lamp:

On

The station is producing boards.

Normal flashing

The station is in production mode, but is not producing boards:

• The machine is waiting for a board to move into the input section.

• The machine is waiting for the output section to become free.

Short flashing

The station has been switched off and cannot currently produce boards because a condition has not been fulfilled:

• Job or setup data must be sent.

• A reference run must be performed.

• The axes must be moved.

Off

The station has been stopped.

White lamps:

There is a white lamp on each side of the machine. These lamps are controlled separately and indicate whether a warning or an alarm has been issued for the corresponding side.

Off

No alarm, no warning.

Short flashing of one lamp

A component provided from several feeder modules is empty on one feeder module or in one level of the MTC/WPC.

Rapid flashing of one lamp

The last feeder module or the last level of the MTC/WPC for a component is being used.

Normal flashing of one lamp

Warning.
Warning from OIS
For a component provided by several feeder modules or MTC/WPC levels:
At least 2 feeder modules are empty and at least 2 feeder modules are still available.

Normal flashing of both lamps

One warning for each side of the machine or one warning for a module that cannot be assigned to one side of the machine.

One lamp on

Alarm on the corresponding side of the machine.

Both lamps on

Alarm on both sides of the machine or for a module that cannot be assigned to one side of the machine or the board fiducial could not be found or recognized.
