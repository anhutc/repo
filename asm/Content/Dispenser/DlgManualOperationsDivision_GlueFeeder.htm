Settings

Temperature control

On/Off.
Temperature regulation is enabled automatically when the setup is changed and disabled when the setup is cleared.

Nominal temperature

The glue can be used when it has reached this temperature.

Measure position of dispenser nozzle

Determines the X, Y and Z position of the tip of the dispenser nozzle.

The glue feeder is only ready for operation after the precise position has been determined.
