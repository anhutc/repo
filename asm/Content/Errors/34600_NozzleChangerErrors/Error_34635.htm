34635 Cannot calibrate specified magazine

At least one magazine of the carrier could not be opened.

Check the mechanical components of the magazine.

The fiducuial is dirty or has adhesive residue on it.

Visual inspection and clean if necessary.

The installed magazine does not match the magazine configuration.

Switch to the view Setup > Nozzle changer and press Magazine configuration. Then either choose the installed magazine type and apply the changed configuration by pressing Apply or install a magazine that matches the configuration shown.
