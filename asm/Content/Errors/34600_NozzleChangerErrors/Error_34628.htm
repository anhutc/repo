34628 Cannot return nozzle on specified segment

Mechanical problem with the nozzle changer.

Visually check the nozzle changer.

Nozzle was removed by hand or lost.

Attach an appropriate nozzle to the specified segment.

Magazine not present.

Place a suitable magazine in the magazine carrier.

The nozzle changer is not calibrated.

Service engineer: Calibrate the nozzle changer.
