35071 Cannot take picture for the orientation check

Axis movement or image capture failed.

Switch to the view Pickup position correction .

If the components are provided in a tray, it may be necessary to refill components to allow them to be reached by the camera.
Use the camera image to check whether the component is at the expected position in the tape or on the waffle pack tray. Note the Pin 1 mark and if necessary, first correct the specified orientation by clicking the +90° / -90° buttons.
Then confirm the orientation of the component by clicking Confirm component orientation.
