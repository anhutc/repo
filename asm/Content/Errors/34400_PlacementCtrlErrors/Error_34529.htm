34529 The interfering edge at placement head is too low

The placement head cannot be moved due to the risk of a crash.

The interfering edge on the placement head corresponds to the bottom edge on the placement head (e.g. component on the nozzle, pin picker, ...).

The specified gap corresponds to the overlap of the top and bottom edges calculated on the basis of the specifications from the programming system.

Check the specifications for the component shape in the programming system (in particular the specified component height), nozzle and pickup location.
