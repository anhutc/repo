35302 Cylinder switch on lifting table faulty or incorrectly adjusted

The cylinder switch is faulty or incorrectly adjusted, i.e. it switches at the wrong position.

The lifting table does not move, faulty air feed.

Check the hoses and the valves of the lifting table.

 

The lifting table moves, but the sensors do not actuate.

Check whether the cables are connected properly.

 

Both light barriers and the cylinder switch are faulty.

Use the Conveyor -> Conveyor functions view to check the correct functioning of the light barriers and, if necessary, replace them.
