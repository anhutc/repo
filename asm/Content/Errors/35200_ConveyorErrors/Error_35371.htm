35371 Moving error width adjustment

The mechanical components do not move freely or are blocked.

Ensure that there is no obstruction in the travel path and carry out maintenance where necessary.

The conveyor rails have collided.

Check the settings for the width adjustment facility. Switch to the view Service > Single calibration > Lane and press Calibrate conveyor rails.

The width adjustment motor is faulty, the motor cable is faulty or not connected.

Service engineer: Check connection of the motor and replace if necessary.

The conveyor controller is faulty.

Service engineer: Replace the conveyor controller.
