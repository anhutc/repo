35385 Error optional stop unit of input section

No compressed air: Faulty valve, faulty compressed air cabling.

Line engineer: Check the compressed air supply and replace the valve if necessary.

The stopper is jammed, dirty or faulty.

Clean the stopper and check for correct operation: Switch to the view Manual operations > Subsystems > Lane > Functions and press On / Off for the input area.

The stopper sensor is faulty.

Replace the stopper.

The stopper cable or the sensor is not connected or is faulty.

Check the connections of the stopper.
