35316 Width adjustment unit: Cannot engage with conveyor edge

The driving plate on the specified width adjustment unit is not able to engage with the conveyor edge.

Driving plate does not rise: Valve of driving plate is faulty.

Replace the valve.

Driving plate does not rise: Fault in the air feed.

Check the air connection and the hoses.

Driving plate rises: Error on cylinder switch.

Replace the cylinder switch.

Actuation element on the conveyor edge is not set correctly, an attempt is made to engage on the wrong side.

Correct the position of the actuation element.
