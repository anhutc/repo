35280 Board is too long

A normal length board is to be assembled: The board in the specified section is too long.

Remove the board that is too long from the machine:

Switch to the view Manual operations > Subsystems > Conveyor lane > Functions and transport the board out of the machine section by section.

A long board is to be assembled: The specified job does not match the long board that has been introduced:

Check the specified job.

2 boards are positioned directly one behind the other.

Check the status on screen: Either remove the second board or push it back.
