Performing maintenance on the valve plunger

Valve plunger for C&P6 head

Valve plunger for C&P12 head

Sleeve

2

3

Sealing lips

  1. Wipe off the valve plunger with a lint-free cloth.

  2. Check the spring O-ring, the sleeve and the sealing lips for damage.

  3. Lightly grease the sealing rings with a lint-free cloth using ISOFLEX TOPAS NCA 52. Make sure that the gaps remain free of grease.

 

If the sleeve, spring O-ring or sealing lips show signs of damage, you must replace the valve plunger.

When you insert the valve plunger, ensure that it moves neither too freely not too tightly. Replace it if this is not the case.
