30356 Holding circuit vacuum too low

The specified value [mbar] must be achieved at least.

Switch to the view Settings > Machine settings and check whether the specified height above sea level corresponds to the location of the mahine. The machine must be restarted after this value is changed.

The hoses for the holding circuit are trapped:

If components are lost during placement (this is also indicated by a Vision error),

check the hose to the vacuum generator.

If the error occurs after placement,

check the measurement hose of the holding circuit.

If vacuum pumps are used: Faulty pump.

Check the vacuum at the manometer of the vacuum pump.
