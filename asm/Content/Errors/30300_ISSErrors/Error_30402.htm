30402 Valve actuator at placement position: switching of valve failed

If the error occurs on one segment:

Valve does not move freely, valve housing dirty.

Perform the maintenance work on the specified segment:

- Clean the valve plunger and the valve housing and grease them again.

- Replace the valve plunger or the valve housing if necessary.

Defective valve actuator for picking up / placement, valve does not move freely, electrical interruption.

Service engineer: Check the electrical connections and replace the valve plungers where necessary.

Light barrier defective, contaminated or incorrectly adjusted.

Check the light barrier and replace it if necessary.

 

If the error occurs on several segments:

Perform the maintenance work on all segments:

- Clean the valve plunger and the valve housing and grease them again.

- Replace the valve plunger or the valve housing if necessary.

Valve actuator not adjusted correctly.

Check the setting with an adjustment valve plunger and readjust the drives.
