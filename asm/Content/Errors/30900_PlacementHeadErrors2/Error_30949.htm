30949 Placement speed reduced because at least one of the Z axes cannot be moved using very low force

At least one segment intended for reduced force Z axis movement cannot be used as it does not move freely.

Caution: This has an impact on placement performance.

Switch to the view Manual operations > Head > Segments and the Height values tab.

The segments identified as OK under "Low force" are suitable for placement, pickup or dipping with reduced force.

Switch to the view Setup > Nozzle changer - Head functions and replace the corresponding nozzles.
