33615 Surface leveling not possible: No placement head found for execution

Either it is not possible to reach all measurement points or no segment is available.

Switch to the view Manual operations > Head > Segments or , rectify the issue that caused the segments to be deactivated and take the segments into operation again.

The measurement points were not taught properly.

Switch to the view Service > Teach machine positions > Inspect positions with gantry n and teach the relevant measurement points again.

Tip: You can find out the names of the measurement points by opening the Leveling assistant - Selection of leveling area and selecting each of the measurement points in turn. Each of the names is displayed below the table.
