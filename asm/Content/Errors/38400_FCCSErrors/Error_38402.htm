38402 Cannot measure position of camera verification plate

It is not possible to measure the position of the camera verification plate (illumination calibration) in the specified processing area.

Check that the 3 fiducials of the camera verification plate are okay and ensure that the camera verification plate was inserted with the arrow pointing in the direction of transport.

 

If the lane is fitted with a pawl stopper, you must use a camera verification plate without chamfered edges.
