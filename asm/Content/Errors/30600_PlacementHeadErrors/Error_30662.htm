30662 At least one segment is disabled

On the specified placement head, one or more segments were disabled by the operator or by the software. No reference run is carried out and no current values are determined for these segments.

Eliminate the cause of deactivation of the segments and take the segments into operation again one after another:

  1. Switch to the view Manual operations > Placement head > Check vacuum, height and scan values, and switch to the tab State overview.

  2. Select the segment.

  3. Press Enable.
