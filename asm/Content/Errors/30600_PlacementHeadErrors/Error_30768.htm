30768 Nozzle comes into contact with component during contactless pickup

The specified position of the Z axis (pickup height) is too low for contactless pickup, with the result that the nozzle comes into contact with the component.

If this error occurs after the tape has been changed or after a change to the thickness of the component:
No action is required as the machine automatically recalibrates the pickup height.

If there is no spacer present, the tape is pressed down when the height is calibrated resulting in determination of a Z position that is too low:

Use a spacer.
