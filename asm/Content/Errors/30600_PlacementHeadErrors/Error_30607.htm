30607 Measured nozzle length too short for specified nozzle type

The actual length of the nozzle is measured after a nozzle change. The value is compared with the type-specific length specified by the line controller. The measured length is outside the permissible tolerance.

Obstruction on the conveyor edge.

Visually inspect the area and remove any obstruction.

Incorrect nozzle type

Change the nozzle as specified.

Nozzle worn or damaged.

Replace the nozzle.
