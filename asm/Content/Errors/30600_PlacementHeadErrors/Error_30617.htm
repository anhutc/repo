30617 Minimum vacuum closed too small for specified nozzle type

Check whether the correct nozzle type is fitted to the specified segment and whether the nozzle has shifted or is damaged.

Valve plunger faulty, leaking or moving too freely.

Replace valve plunger.

Compressed air circuit between compressed air unit and placement head interrupted.

Notify line engineer: Replace kinked pneumatic hoses. Hoses in vicinity of head not airtight. Check screw fittings and tighten if necessary.

Silencer dirty or not properly maintained.

Notify line engineer: Fit a new filter in the silencer.
