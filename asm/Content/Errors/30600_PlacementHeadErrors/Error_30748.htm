30748 Invalid calibrated vacuum value with closed nozzle

The measured vacuum value is either greater than the minimum vacuum value (database table for vacuum and forced air parameters), or is less than the physically achievable vacuum of -1000 mbar.

Check the nozzle or hose for leaks.

If the machine is located more than 500m above sea level, it may be necessary to modify the parameters in the machine database.

Contact your service provider.
