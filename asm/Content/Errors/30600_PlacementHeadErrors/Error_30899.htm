30899 Difference in vacuum too big between placement and pick-up

Valve plunger dirty or worn.

Replace the valve plunger.

Valve housing dirty.

Clean the valve housing.

Distributor disk not airtight.

Check the distributor disk and replace it if necessary.

If the error occurs on several segments:

The vacuum values prior to pickup are greater than after placement: incorrectly calibrated valve actuator.

Readjust the valve actuator using the adjustment valve.

Distributor or distributor disk dirty or worn.

Clean the distributor and distributor disk, check the O rings for damage and replace if necessary.

Overall vacuum too low or irregular.

Clean the vacuum system and hoses, replace the venturi nozzles and silencers.

Vacuum measuring board faulty.

Replace the affected vacuum measuring board.

Vacuum generator faulty.

Replace the vacuum generator.
