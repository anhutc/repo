30660 Magazine has been removed

The placement head and gantry must not be moved during this operation.

A magazine has inadvertently been pulled from its mounting when replacing a nozzle in the nozzle changer.

Open the protective cover, remove the nozzle changer magazine carefully from the segment and replace it in the nozzle changer. Check that the nozzle is seated properly and check that it can also be removed from the sleeve.
