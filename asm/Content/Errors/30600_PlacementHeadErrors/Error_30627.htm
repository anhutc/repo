30627 Nozzle still on segment after attempt to return nozzle

There is already a nozzle in the garage.

Remove this nozzle from the garage by hand.

Nozzle changer cannot be locked.

Check the locking mechanism.

Nozzle changer or segment offset not calibrated.

Notify line engineer: Switch to the "Service" view, press the Automatic calibration button and choose Nozzle changer and Head and cameras.

Filter disk (between nozzle and sleeve) is no longer seated correctly. It may be worn after a long period of use and has dropped.

Try to press the filter disk firmly into its seat again. If this is not possible, the filter disk must be replaced.
