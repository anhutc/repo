30804 Component was not placed properly because Z axis light barrier triggered unexpectedly

The Z axis has traveled lower than expected. The component may have been damaged in the process.

Ensure that the board is secured firmly and check its planarity.

In the programming system, check the height of the component as described and the distance to be maintained for touchless placement (Touchless Placement, Z distance).

 

For more detailed information, see also the messages in the error chain.
