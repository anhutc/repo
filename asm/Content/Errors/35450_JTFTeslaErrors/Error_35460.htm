35460 No tray in specified level

Make sure that a cassette is set up as specified.

Switch to the view Service data for the Multiple JEDEC Tray Feeder.

In the Refill position drop-down menu, press Move to refill position for the relevant cassette. Open the loading door and pull out the cassette. Place the correct tray in the specified level. Push the cassette back into the tower and close the loading door. In the Refill positiondrop-down menu, press Quit refill position.
