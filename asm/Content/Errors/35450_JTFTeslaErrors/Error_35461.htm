35461 Unexpected tray on conveyor

The conveyor of the JTF-ML2 is not free.

An attempt is being made to push a tray onto the conveyor, but another tray is still there.

Remove the waffle pack tray or other obstacle from the conveyor by hand or switch to the view Location > X-Table > JTF-ML 2 and press Move tower into transport position.

The tray on the conveyor is moved back into the tower. You can then provide the required tray.
