33518 Cannot calibrate centering station

For more detailed information, refer to the messages in the error chain.

 

The centering and reject station must be calibrated to allow nozzles to be calibrated.

Restart calibration of the centering and reject station:

  1. Open the "Service" view and click the Single calibration button.

  2. In the "Service > Single calibration" view, click the Calibrate nozzle changer button for the required nozzle changer. This opens the Calibrate nozzle changers dialog box.

  3. Click the Single selection button.

  4. Click the button or select the corresponding entry (RS stands for Reject Station) from the table.
