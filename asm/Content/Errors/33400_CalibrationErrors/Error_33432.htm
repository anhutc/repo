33432 Cannot calibrate offset between glass plate camera and board camera

Camera: Flip chip camera (SST 47) - Stationary board camera for measuring the relative positions of the top and bottom sides of the component

A calibration step cannot be performed because the glass plate of the stationary camera is dirty.

Clean the glass plate and make sure that it is not scratched or cracked and restart calibration.

The specified calibration tool cannot be picked up.

Visually check whether the calibration tool has been lost and is lying in the machine.
Insert the appropriate calibration tool for the sensor system type into the corresponding calibration tool pocket in such a way that the printed

fiducials face upwards.

The calibration tool is dirty or damaged.

Clean the calibration tool and replace it if necessary.

Stationary camera: The dichroic mirror is dirty.

Service engineer: Clean the camera.
