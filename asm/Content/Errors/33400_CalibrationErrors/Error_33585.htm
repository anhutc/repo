33585 Z height has to be calibrated, camera

A calibration step cannot be performed because the Z height of the specified camera has not yet been calibrated.

Calibrate the camera and then start the aborted calibration step again:

  1. Open the "Service" view and click the Single calibration button.
  2. In the "Service > Single calibration" view , click the Calibrate head button for the required head. The dialog box Service > Single calibration > Gantry n > Head opens.
  3. Click the Calibrate head cameras and all segments button. Calibration of the entire head will now be performed. Values are determined for the camera coefficient, the camera angle and the segment offsets.
  4. Click Save to save the data determined during calibration in the machine data.
