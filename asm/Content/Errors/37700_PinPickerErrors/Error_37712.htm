37712 Downward movement of the pin picker was stopped unexpectedly

The lower sensor is not covered.

The pin picker has encountered an obstacle:
An attempt has been made to replace a support pin in a garage that already contains a pin or to put down a pin on the lifting table.

Remove the support pin from the garage or remove the obstacle from the lifting table.

The lower sensor is faulty.

Service engineer: Replace the lowest sensor as described in the Service Manual.

The cylinder valve is faulty.

Replace the cylinder valve.

The Z guide of the pin picker is faulty.

Service engineer: Replace the entire mechanical unit for Smart Pin Support.
