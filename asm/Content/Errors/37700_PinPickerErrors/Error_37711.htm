37711 CAUTION: Do not move the gantry by hand! The pin picker is not in a safe position

The upper sensor is not covered.

The cylinder is jammed.

Attempts to release the pin picker by hand.

The upper sensor is faulty or the cylinder valve is not switching.

Service engineer: Switch to the view Manual operations > Subsystems > Gantry x > Pin picker > Functions > Pin picker functions taband click Z axis -> Down to check that the cylinder valve is working. If necessary, replace the sensor or the cylinder valve as described in the Service Manual.
