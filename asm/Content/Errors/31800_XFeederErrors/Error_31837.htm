31837 Foil torn at specified track. Machine was stopped to protect Z axis

If the foil tears, there is a risk of damaging the component sensor of the C&P20 head.

The machine therefore stops and all feeders on the location are deactivated until the tear in the foil has been eliminated.

 

The foil is torn or not tensioned correctly.

Check the foil. Press the yellow button until the foil is tensioned correctly.

The feeder is to be removed.

Press the BACK button on the X feeder for at least 2 seconds.
