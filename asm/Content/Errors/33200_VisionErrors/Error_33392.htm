33392 Calibration part missing or damaged (872)

The calibration tool is missing or is in the incorrect orientation on the camera verification plate.

Ensure that the calibration tools are positioned in the correct orientation at the specified positions on the camera verification plate.

The calibration tool is dirty or damaged.

Clean the calibration tool and replace it if necessary.

Stationary camera: The dichroic mirror is dirty.

Service engineer: Clean the camera.
