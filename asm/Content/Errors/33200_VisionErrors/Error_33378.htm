33378 Camera cannot be addressed (242)

The signal path between the SIPLACE Vision PC and the camera is interrupted.

PCB/component cameras:

If both cameras have failed:

Check the cabling on the SIPLACE Vision PC.
Check the coaxial wires with the burgundy and blue connectors on the trailing cable.

If only one of the cameras has failed:

Service engineer: Replace the affected camera.

FC/IC cameras:

Check the cabling on the SIPLACE Vision PC.

Check the cabling on the camera (IC camera cabling confused with FC camera cabling?).

If this does not rectify the problem:

Service engineer: Replace the camera.
