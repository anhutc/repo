33261 Not all connections have been fluxed (838)

Ball connections damaged or dirty.

Inspect the batch of components.

Unsuitable or insufficient flux.

Check the parameter settings for the dipping unit to ensure that the fluxing quantity is high enough.

Component badly positioned on the nozzle or not parallel with the dipping unit

In the programming system, check that the correct nozzle has been assigned to the component and check the pickup position.

Dipping time too short.

Check the specifications in the programming system.
