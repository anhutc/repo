33373 3D measurement: At least one connection is damaged (882)

For at least one connection, insufficient quality was determined during measurement.

Check the component description (SIPLACE Vision).

  • In the "Topographic image", check whether the component is in the measuring range of the 3D sensor. Select the pseudo-color image to do this. The connections must appear as green through red. If this is not the case, check the component height in the programming system.

     

  • In the "Topographic image" again, check whether the contact surfaces of the connections have relatively large dark spots. This is an indication that the surface of the connections is too reflective. Increase the exposure time (algorithm parameter).
