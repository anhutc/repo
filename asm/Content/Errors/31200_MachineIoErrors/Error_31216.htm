31216 Machine has stopped: Ballast resistor too hot

The fan is not working.

First ensure that all the boards still in the machine are completed:

Lock the input conveyor to prevent any more boards from entering the machine. Switch to the Production view and click .

To complete assembly of boards still in the machine:

Press the Start button. The machine operates for a short time. Repeat this process until all the boards have been cleared from the machine.

The ballast resistor is covered by dust and lint or the fan is dirty.

Line engineer or service engineer: Switch off the machine and disconnect it from the power supply. Clean the fan and the resistor with a vacuum cleaner. Switch the machine on again and check whether the fan runs.

The fan is faulty.

Service engineer: Replace it.
