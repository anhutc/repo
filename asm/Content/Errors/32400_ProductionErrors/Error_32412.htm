32412 Place position must be inspected

A previous error means that it is not possible to determine accurately whether the placement position was placed or not.

Check the placement position and enter its status:

  1. Click the icon for the relevant board in the main view .
    The "Board recovery placement" view is opened.

  2. Select the placement position, click Show image and check whether the position has already been placed in the camera image.

  3. Then click Placed or Not placed.

  4. Click Finish board to continue placement.
