32589 The calculated board angle is outside tolerance with the selected positions

The board rotation cannot be determined with the selected positions

Two points joined by a line parallel to either the longitudinal or lateral edge of the board must be selected. The two points selected must be at a sufficient distance from each other.

The line connecting the two currently selected points is a line that cannot be parallel to any side of the board. The calculated angle deviation is greater than the internally defined tolerance threshold.

Select suitable points in the Vision view and press Apply positions for compensating board rotation and continue.

Hint: Choose two features on the board (e.g. fiducials or long conductor tracks or other lines) that share the same X or Y coordinate, i.e. that are parallel with the X or Y axis and that are as far apart as possible.
