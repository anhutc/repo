37469 Common FFI communication error

Reactivate the relevant track.

Switch to the view Setup > X-Table , select the relevant track and click Details... Then click Activate with current settings.

If problem still exists:

Depending on the feeder type:

- Switch the feeder off and on again and then activate it.

- Remove the feeder and replace it.

 

Replace the feeder if necessary.

 

Contact your service provider or the feeder manufacturer.
