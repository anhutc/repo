39817 Support pin on lifting table out of tolerance

The specified support pin is not at the specified position.

Switch to the view Setup > Support pins in processing area and click Returned to magazines.

Check whether any pins are damaged, dirty or have any adhesive residue on them and whether components are stuck to the foot. Replace or clean the support pins as necessary and return them to the pin magazine.

Then click Scan pin magazines.

Make sure that there are no components on the lifting table.
