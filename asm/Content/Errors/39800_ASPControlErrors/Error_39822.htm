39822 Downloaded support pin positions are not allowed

The job from the programming system could not be accepted because at least one support pin could not be placed at the specified position.

Possible causes:

•

The support pin is too close to another support pin or too close to a conveyor rail.

•

There is a hole in the board or a screw at the relevant position, so that the support pin cannot be placed.

•

The position is outside the travel range of the pin picker.

Check the specification at the programming system and specify the job again.
