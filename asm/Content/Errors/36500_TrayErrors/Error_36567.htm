36567 Error on moving waffle pack tray carrier in or out

Magnet on bottom of waffle pack tray carrier.

Check the waffle pack tray carrier and the feed axis driving plate.

An error occurred when moving the waffle pack tray carrier.

Check that the sensors that determine whether waffle pack tray carriers are in the tower or whether a waffle pack tray carrier has been provided are working properly.

If the waffle pack tray has not been set up,

Perform a reference run:

Switch to the view Manual operations > Subsystems > MTC > Axis properties , choose the relevant feed axis and click Perform reference run.
