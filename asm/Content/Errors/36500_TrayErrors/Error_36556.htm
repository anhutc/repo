36556 Zero point offset by one revolution of the motor

The zero point of the feed axis has been moved by one revolution of the motor. This shift has been corrected automatically.

If this shift occurs regularly then:

Service engineer: Release the drive belt of the motor and move the cog by approximately 10 teeth.

Switch to the view Manual operations >  Subsystems >  MTC >  Teach axes , choose the relevant feed axis and start the teach operation. Teach zero position.
