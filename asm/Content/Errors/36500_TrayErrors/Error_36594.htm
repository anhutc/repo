36594 Safety flap opening time exceeded

The electromagnet that opens and closes the safety flap is faulty or not connected properly.

Check the electromagnet and replace it if necessary.

Mechanical obstruction.

Ensure that the safety flap is not obstructed (e.g. by a waffle pack tray carrier).

The sensor showing that the safety flap is open is not connected.

Ensure that the sensor is connected properly.

The sensor is not adjusted correctly.

Service engineer: Adjust the sensor in such a way that it triggers properly when the flap is opened.
