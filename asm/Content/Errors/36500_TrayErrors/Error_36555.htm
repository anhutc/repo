36555 Zero point correction of feed axis failed, zero point could not be corrected

It has been identified that the zero point of the feed axis on the specified tower has been moved. The zero point has not been corrected automatically.

Service engineer: Check the zero point and set it again:

Switch to the view Manual operations >  Subsystems >  MTC >  Teach axes , choose the relevant feed axis and start the teach operation. Teach zero position.
