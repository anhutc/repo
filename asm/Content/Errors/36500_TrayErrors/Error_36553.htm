36553 Setup error, waffle pack tray carrier missing

One or more waffle pack tray carriers specified in the set-up are not available in the specified tower.

Switch to the view for the MTC , click Refill position > Stop refilling position for tower x. Then click Occupancy check > Check WPTC occupancy for tower x.

The missing levels are shown in a dialog box.

Push the relevant waffle pack tray carriers into the MTC.
