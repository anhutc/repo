34942 Pickup location deactivated until component at pickup position is teached

For this pickup location, it has been specified that the surface of the component is to be inspected before each pickup operation.

Switch to the view Setup > Teach component shape , select the appropriate pickup position (Position) and press Test component at pickup position to check and, if necessary, change the pickup position on the basis of the surface structures of the selected component.

After teaching, switch to the view Setup > Deactivated tracks, choose the appropriate entry and press Selected track > Enable.
