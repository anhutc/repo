34945 Tray deactivated because at least one component exceeds the maximum permitted pickup height

The WPC is not aligned correctly.

Service engineer: Align the WPC so that it is level using the spirit level on the feed axis.

Tray carrier warped.

Remove the tray carrier and replace it if necessary.

The tray is warped or there are components or other debris underneath the tray.

Remove the tray carrier, lift out the tray and clean the underside or replace the tray.

The tray contains incorrect components, components are on edge in the tray or there is debris beneath the component.

Remove the tray carrier and check the components in the relevant tray.
