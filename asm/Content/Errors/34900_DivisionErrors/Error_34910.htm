34910 Pickup location deactivated because of torn foil

If the foil tears, there is a risk of damaging the component sensor of the C&P20 head. The machine therefore stops until the tear in the foil has been eliminated.

The foil on a different feeder is torn or not tensioned correctly (red LED of this feeder is illuminated).

Check the foil of the feeder affected. Press the yellow button until the foil is tensioned correctly.
