34939 Wrong cassette configuration detected

Only 2 cassettes with 7 levels each or 2 cassettes with 9 levels each are allowed to be present.

The following constellations are incorrect:

- 2 cassettes with 7 levels, although cassettes with 9 levels are required.

- 2 cassettes with 9 levels, although cassettes with 7 levels are required.

- 1 cassette with 7 levels, and one cassette with 9 levels.

Switch to the view Setup > Location > X-Table > JTF-ML 2 and replace the incorrect cassette as specified.
