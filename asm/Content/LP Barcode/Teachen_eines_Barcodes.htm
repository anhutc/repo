Teaching a barcode

The size and position of a barcode for a board or a panel are defined in SIPLACE Pro. Before a barcode can be evaluated, the barcode type must be specified. An output format for the barcode can then be defined.

 

Proceed as follows to teach a barcode:

  1. Open the view Board > Teach fiducials.

  2. Select a conveyor lane.
    All the barcodes specified in the panel for this conveyor lane are shown in the list.

  3. Select the appropriate entry from the list:
    Click directly on the entry or select the entry using the arrow keys on the keyboard.

  4. Place a board in the selected conveyor.

  5. Click the Test selected fiducial button.
    SIPLACE Vision is started and the board is moved to the relevant position.

  6. Click the "Geometry" icon .
    (This view is started automatically if the barcode descriptions are incomplete.)

  7. Specify the barcode type:
    Select the required entry for the Barcode type parameter.

  8. Change the relevant parameters as necessary.
    Adjust the size, for instance, if the camera image shows that the barcode does not lie completely within the field of view of the camera.

  9. Click the New measurement button to show the raw barcode data.

  10. Define filters to specify the output format for the barcode.

  11. Click Save to send the changed barcode data to SIPLACE Pro and thus to all other stations in the line.
