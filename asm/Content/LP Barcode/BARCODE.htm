Board barcode

If a barcode for a board or for panels is located on the top of the board, it can be scanned with the board camera. If the barcode is larger than the field of view of the camera, this can be overcome with multiple measurements.

The size and position of a barcode are defined in SIPLACE Pro. A fiducial of the type "PCB barcode" is described here. The specified barcode is shown in the fiducial list at the station. A barcode must be taught in SIPLACE Vision and the output format must be defined before it can be completely evaluated for traceability purposes.

The changes made in SIPLACE Vision are sent to SIPLACE Pro and distributed to all machines in the line.
