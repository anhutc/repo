Minimum recognition quality

The recognition quality corresponds to the ratio edge points found : edge points searched for.

The specified threshold must be exceeded if the fiducial is to be flagged as recognized.

The following applies for ink spots: If the proportion of edge points found is above the threshold, the subpanel is assembled, otherwise it is omitted.

 

Default: 0.7 -> at least 70 % of the edge points that the system searches for must be found.

Recommended setting: 0.5 through 0.9.

  • Reduction of the value:
    Reducing the threshold means that poorly defined fiducials will still be recognized.

    Caution: There is an increased risk of structures being confused and the robustness of position recognition suffering if the threshold is lowered.
