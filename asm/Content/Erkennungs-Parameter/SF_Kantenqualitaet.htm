Edge quality

The edge quality value defines the requirements on the edge points. If the edges can be recognized as precise lines, they have a high quality.

1: very good edge quality, the edge points are in line.
If edge points deviate from this, they are not taken into account when calculating the position of the fiducial or the substrate.

5: poor edge quality, the edge is "rough".

 

  • Reduction of the edge quality, i.e. increase in this value:
    More points are taken into account when calculating the position of the fiducial or the substrate. The recognition quality improves, but the process is more sensitive to artifacts.
