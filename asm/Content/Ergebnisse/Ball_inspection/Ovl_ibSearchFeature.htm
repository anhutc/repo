Recognition step: Features searched for (inspection)

 

Light blue

The features searched for (corresponds to the ball/column description).

Filter points

(yellow and dark blue points):

These must lie on the sides of the corners or on the contours of the feature that has been described as a "Pattern".

Light features:

Blue filter points

Must lie on the connections.

Yellow filter points

Must lie outside the connection surfaces.

Dark features:

Blue filter points

Must lie outside the connection surfaces.

Yellow filter points

Must lie on the connection surfaces.

Edge points
(orange points)

If balls or columns are located very close to each other, no point pairs are shown. Instead, only points close to the edge of the balls / columns are shown.

Position of the center of the component in the model.
