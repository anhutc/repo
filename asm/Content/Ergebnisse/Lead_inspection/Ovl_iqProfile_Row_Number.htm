Recognition step: Lead position in group ... (component inspection)

Search for the lead center positions of all leads in the selected group:

Green curve

The height of the curve shows the brightness of a lead. If a lead is too dark or the curve is too low, it is not recognized (internal threshold).

Orange dash

Center of lead.

Red rectangle

Marks the lead group which is represented.

You may be able to adapt the illumination settings with this view.

The effects of changing the illuminations are shown clearly here.
