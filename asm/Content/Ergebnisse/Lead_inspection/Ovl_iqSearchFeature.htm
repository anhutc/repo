Recognition step: Features searched for (component inspection)

During component inspection, all features are inspected.

Light blue

The features searched for.

Filter point pairs
(yellow and dark blue points):

These must lie on the sides of the corners or on the contours of the feature that has been described as a "Pattern".

Light features:

Blue filter points

Must lie on the connections.

Yellow filter points

Must lie outside the connection surfaces.

Dark features:

Blue filter points

Must lie outside the connection surfaces.

Yellow filter points

Must lie on the connection surfaces.

Detected position of the component center: All leads were recognized.

Detected position of the component center: Not all leads were recognized.

 

Problems:

  • The component is located outside the search area:
    Check the pickup position and the pickup tolerance specification (packaging tolerance in the programming system).
  • The yellow frame is only just larger than the component:
    Check the packaging tolerance and reduce the tolerances to the minimum necessary.
