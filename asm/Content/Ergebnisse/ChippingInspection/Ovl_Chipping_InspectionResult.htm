Chipping inspection: Edge analysis

Results of the measurements along the edges:

Blue line Nominal edge.
Yellow line

"Tolerance for depth of chipped area" threshold:

If the value at the relevant point is larger than the specified threshold this may be a chipping defect.

Color highlighting for individual points along the outer edges:

 

Green highlighting

The measured distance from the nominal edge is below the "Tolerance for depth of chipped area" threshold.

 

Orange highlighting

The measured distance from the nominal edge is above the "Tolerance for depth of chipped area" threshold, but the "Tolerance for length of chipped area" threshold was not exceeded.

 

Red highlighting

The measured distance from the nominal edge is above the "Tolerance for depth of chipped area" threshold, and the "Tolerance for length of chipped area" threshold was exceeded along the edge. The height of the bar corresponds to the discrepancy from the nominal edge.

A chipped area is detected on the edge!

Red rectangle

The length and width correspond to the maximum length and maximum depth of a chipped area.
