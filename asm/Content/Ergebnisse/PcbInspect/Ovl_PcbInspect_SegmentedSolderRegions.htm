Recognition step: Evaluation of solder deposits

Shows all the solder deposits in the inspection area:

Yellow

Border around the solder deposit in the model.

Green

Border around the current solder deposit.

The more accurately the green marking overlays the yellow marking, the better the result.

Green: The solder deposit is okay.

Magenta

Indicates a solder bridge.

Red

A single solder deposit is faulty.

A separate comparison image can be shown for each red solder deposit.
