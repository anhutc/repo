Recognition step: Solder defect comparison

Comparison of individual faulty solder deposits: Solder deposit in model - Solder deposit on current board.

A solder deposit is identified as faulty if at least one threshold is exceeded:

•

The height of the current solder deposit differs from the height of the solder deposit in the model by more than the specified threshold (Maximum height difference for solder deposits).

•

The volume of the current solder deposit differs from the volume of the solder deposit in the model by more than the specified percentage (Maximum or minimum volume difference for solder deposits).

Note:

If errors occur frequently when comparing heights of BGA components, in particular, check whether the learned heights in the model fluctuate significantly. If this is the case, enable Average solder height as reference.
