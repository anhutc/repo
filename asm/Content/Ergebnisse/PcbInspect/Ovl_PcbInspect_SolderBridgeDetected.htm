Recognition step: Solder bridge detected

Each solder deposit is assigned a specific area in which it must be found. The area at the edge must not be covered with solder, as it could otherwise melt into the next solder deposit (solder bridge).

Red

Solder bridge:

Solder that belongs to the solder deposit was found at this point in the area around the edge.

 

Example:

The solder deposit is not clearly delineated. Solder has also been detected at the edge.

The solder deposit is offset to such an extent that a solder bridge could be formed with the neighboring solder deposit.
