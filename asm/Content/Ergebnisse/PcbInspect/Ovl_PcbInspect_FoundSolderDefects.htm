Recognition step: Results of solder deposit inspection

Shows a 3D view of the current height map:

Marked in red

 

 

 

 

All faulty solder deposits:

Faulty:

•

 The height of the current solder deposit differs from the height of the solder deposit in the model by more than the specified threshold. Parameter: Maximum height difference for solder deposits.

•

The volume of the current solder deposit differs from the volume of the solder deposit in the model by more than the specified percentage. Parameter: Maximum volume difference for solder deposits or Minimum volume difference for solder deposits.

Not covered:

•

Less than 30 % of the model solder deposit are covered with solder paste in the current solder deposit.

Marked in magenta

 

Solder bridges:

•

The solder deposit is not clearly delineated. Solder paste has also been detected at the edge of the neighboring solder deposit.
