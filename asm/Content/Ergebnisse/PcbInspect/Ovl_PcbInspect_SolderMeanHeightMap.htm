Recognition step: Average height of solder deposits

Shows the learned average heights of the individual solder deposits.

The average height is calculated for each solder deposit in the model. The solder deposit is shown in proportion to its height.

Examples:

The solder deposits are shown in 3D:

• The base area corresponds to the calculated area of the solder deposit.

• The height corresponds to the calculated average height of the solder deposit.
