Recognition step: Y gradient (fine) - components without leads

Representation of the edge information for fine position determination (edges in Y direction):

Cyan curve

Representation of edge positions and edge quality in the form of a gradient.

The peaks in this curve indicate the grayscale transitions (indication of component edges).

Pink double-headed arrow

Orientation of gradient formation.

Gray rectangle

Area over which the gradient is generated (depends on component size, component tolerance and packaging tolerance).
