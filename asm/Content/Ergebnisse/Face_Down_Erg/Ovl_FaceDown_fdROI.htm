Recognition step: Region of interest for detecting flipped components

 

Brightness method:

The system proposes an area in the middle of the body of the component. The brightness value determined in the region of interest is specified on the component.

Contrast method:

The system checks whether different intensities are present in the suggested area.

Detection using gullwing connections:

The system proposes an area at the inner end of the lead (width = lead width, length = lead length - contact length, distance from component body = 0).

Detection using gullwing connections with illumination from one side:

The row of leads closer to the light source is defined as the reference row (marked in yellow).
