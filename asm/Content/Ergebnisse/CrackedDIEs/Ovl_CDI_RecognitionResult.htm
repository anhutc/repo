Recognition step: Detection of cracked components (cracked die inspection)

Result of the cracked die inspection if a crack or fracture was identified.

Red line

Indicates a crease in the die.

Area with red outline

Indicates a crack in the die.

 

Examples:

Die is creased/fractured.

The die has a crack.
