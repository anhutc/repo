Recognition step: Region of interest for detection of cracked components (cracked die inspection)

The system searches the region of interest for differences in brightness in order to determine whether a cracked die is present in the tape or the tray.

Yellow rectangle

Region of interest (ROI):
This region is used to detect any crack or crease.

 

Result of this step:

"No crack or crease detected in component".
