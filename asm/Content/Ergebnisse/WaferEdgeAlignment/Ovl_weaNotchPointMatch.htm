Substrate: Notch detection (point pair filtering)

This additional recognition step is carried out if notch detection by contour tracing is unsuccessful.

The objective of this method is to identify positions along the external contour of the substrate at which the contour shows a kink.

This kink is the notch and is described by the depth, width and curvature of the notch. This description is used to create a point pair filter that is moved along the edge.

A kink is deemed to have been found at the position at which most point pairs lie in such a way that the yellow points are in the light region and the blue points are in the dark region.
