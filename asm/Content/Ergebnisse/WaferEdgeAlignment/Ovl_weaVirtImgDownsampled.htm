Substrate: Virtual camera image (scaled down)

Shows the regions along the outer edge of the substrate used to determine the exact X/Y position of the substrate:

As a rule, 3 edge points are needed in order to determine the position.

Blue rectangles

Evaluation positions on the edge of the substrate. Used to determine the exact X/Y position of the substrate.

The numbers shown correspond to the names of each of the evaluation positions.

Light blue rectangles

Evaluation position for the notch.
