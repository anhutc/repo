Recognition step: Fine step, current position

During teaching, line segments were identified in the training image.

The centers of these line segments are now to be detected in the fine step. This is done be comparing the grayscale values of the line segment centers in the model with those of the camera image at the current position. The current position is updated using an iterative optimization method.

 

To enhance performance, this process is performed twice, firstly at reduced resolution and then at full resolution.

4 interim results are displayed:

  • Start position of the fine step at reduced resolution
  • End position of the fine step at reduced resolution
  • Start position of the fine step at full resolution
  • The detected fiducial position

 

Red points

The centers of the line segments. These should be at the outer edge of the fiducial.

Green rectangle

Region of interest:

Presentation position + size of fiducial

 

Line segments: straight edges a few pixels in length which are ideally detected along the outer contour of the fiducial and taken together, describe the fiducial completely.
