Recognition step: Coarse step position(s)

Verification of the correlation between the model and the camera image.

The position at which the greatest correlation was detected in the last step is investigated first. Then the position is investigated at which the second greatest correlation was detected, etc.

If the quality value determined is above a particular threshold, this step is canceled and the determined position is used as the starting position for the subsequent fine steps.

 

Black point with white frame:

Position of reference point determined in the coarse step.

White frame without black point:

The system attempted to find the reference point at this position without success.
