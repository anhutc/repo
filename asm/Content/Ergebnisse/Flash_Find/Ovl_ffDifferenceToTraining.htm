Recognition step: Difference from training image

Brightness range: 0..255

O (black in the image)

In the training image, the pixel is shown as black, but the corresponding pixel in the camera image is white, i.e. there is no correlation.

127

The pixels in the training image and the camera image are identical: maximum correlation.

255

In the training image, the pixel is shown as white, but the corresponding pixel in the camera image is black, i.e. there is no correlation.

 

If the fiducial is clearly identified, all values should be around 127, i.e. a homogeneous gray image (high correlation at all points).
