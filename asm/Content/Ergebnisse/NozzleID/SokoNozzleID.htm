Soko nozzle ID

Data sets for special nozzles can be imported into the programming system. Nozzle numbers can be assigned to these nozzles.

The special nozzles have a black protected coating (anodized). Large and small circular markings are laser-etched into this coating.

The search area is divided into three sectors of equal size, beginning at the start marking (bar). Two large circles and three small circles must be present in each of these sectors. These circles must be separated by two wide gaps and three narrow gaps in each sector.
