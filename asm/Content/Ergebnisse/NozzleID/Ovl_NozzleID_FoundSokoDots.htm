Recognition step: Detection of Soko code (nozzle ID)

Check whether a Soko nozzle (special design) is present:

The system checks whether sufficient large and small circular markings are found at a given distance from the center.

 

Blue

Large circular marking detected:

Turquoise

Small circular marking detected:

Green

The radius of this circle matches the distance the detected circular markings should be from the center.

Example:

All circular markings are present.

The distance between the 1st and 2nd circular markings (anticlockwise from the start marking) is too large. A circular marking is missing.
