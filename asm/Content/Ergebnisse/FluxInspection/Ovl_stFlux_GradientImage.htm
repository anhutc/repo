Recognition step: Flux inspection - gradient (homogeneity analysis)

Representation of the change in brightness within a single ball.

The changes in brightness are best displayed in the pseudo-color image: The greater the change, the brighter the image and hence the greater the characteristic value of a ball.

This means that the more homogeneous the brightness is across a ball, the lower its characteristic value.

 

Yellow

Shows the region of interest that has been set.

1 yellow circle: The area inside the circle will be evaluated.

2 yellow circles: The area between the two circles will be evaluated.

 

Low characteristic value, the ball is deemed to have been dipped.

High characteristic value, the ball is deemed to have been insufficiently dipped.
