Nozzle: Dirty socket

With some nozzles (type 65xx), the system also searches for soiling in the vicinity of the nozzle and the socket.

Bright areas indicate soiling (e.g. with solder paste).

Red

Identified edge of a dirty area.

 

Examples:

The diameter of the overall area of all dirty patches exceeds the specified threshold (maximum limit).

The nozzle is dirty and must be cleaned.

Nozzle OK.

Definition of the upper limit:

The size of the narrower side of the smallest component that can be placed by the nozzle corresponds to the upper limit.
