Nozzle scanning: Determined suction ducts

Analysis of the suction ducts of nozzles on the basis of the detected brightness of structures.

The determined edge of the suction ducts is indicated in color:

Green

The suction duct is clear.

Red

The suction duct is blocked.

 

Examples:

The outer suction ducts are blocked.

The camera is dirty.

Note:

If the duct appears to be okay and if the error recurs with different nozzles, this is an indication that the camera is dirty at the point at which the suction duct is not recognized.
