Recognition step: Coarse angle - components without leads

Determination of the rough rotation angle of the component:

White line

Rough rotation of the component (coarse angle).
If the coarse angle and the fine angle are the same, the white line is covered by the green line.

Green line

Exact determined rotation of the component (fine angle).

Yellow rectangle

Search area in which the component must be located.
