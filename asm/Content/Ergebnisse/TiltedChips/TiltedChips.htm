Tilted chip components

When chip components are picked up, it is possible that individual components are picked up incorrectly by the nozzle. It is then possible that the component is tilted on the nozzle and may slip during placement: .

If the vision system detects this, the component is rejected in order to avoid incorrect placement.

 

If detection of tilted components is enabled, an additional image of the component is captured using steep 0° illumination and the brightness of the connections is inspected:

The steep 0° illumination is reflected back to the camera by the metallic connections.

The connections can be seen easily.

 

The component was picked up correctly.

The steep 0° illumination is partly reflected sideways by the metallic connections.

The connections only appear bright close to the side edge of the component.

The more a chip component is tilted, the more only one side of the component can be seen clearly.

The leads appear dark (low calculated brightness).

 

The component is tilted.

 

Proceed as follows to enable detection of tilted components:

  • Switch to the Measurement options view.
  • Choose Detection of tilted chip components and press Enable measurement option.
