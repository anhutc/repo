Recognition step: Check connection lengths

 

Cyan curve

Gradient of the grayscale profile in X.

Locations with a significant change in brightness are represented as spikes in the gradient.

Yellow frame

The ends of the connection that are to the inside of the component must be located within this area.

Pale blue vertical lines

These indicate the locations at which there are significant transitions between light and dark, and also the boundaries of the component.

Green vertical lines

Locations at which there are significant transitions between light and dark were found, but they could not be unambiguously recognized.

Magenta vertical lines

These indicate the locations at which there are significant transitions between light and dark within the connections.

If the system detects such locations, the component is rejected.

Orange lines

These mark the positive and negative threshold values.

Only those light/dark transitions that exceed these threshold values are recognized as component boundaries.
