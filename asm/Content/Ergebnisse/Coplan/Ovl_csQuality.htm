3D sensor: Measurement quality

The measurement quality is a measure of the reliability of the measured values. It describes the ratio found measuring points : expected measuring points.

The calculated quality value is entered in the representation of the features.
To identify the features, you must zoom into the image.

 

Quality value specification (0 - 100)

0: poor

100: good

Green

Good quality.

Green through yellow

The feature quality tends toward good.

Yellow through red

The feature quality tends toward poor.

Red

Poor quality.

Red cross

The feature was not recognized.

Mauve cross (balls only)

The ball is damaged. The tip of the ball may be flattened or pointed rather than spherical.

Yellow rectangle

Component body.

 

Hint:

  • Check the topographic image if red or magenta crosses are shown.
    If data points are missing on parts of a connection (e.g. black spots on the top of a BGA ball), you can increase the exposure time.
  • In the Measure height view, check whether the component was measured on the focal plane of the sensor.
