Coplanarity measurement: Measurement with 3D sensor

Measurement can be carried out with the 3D sensor for some component types. The specification as to whether it is possible to carry out measurement with the 3D sensor is made in the programming system.

 

BGAs:

With BGA components, the height of the individual balls is determined and then checked to ensure that the measured heights meet the coplanarity requirements.

 

With components with gullwing leads (without notch), the position of the individual placement planes is determined and checked to ensure that the discrepancies between the measured lead heights are within the specified tolerances.
