3D sensor: Coplanarity and Coplanarity (values)

 

Shows the coplanarity discrepancy between the component connections and the placement plane.

The calculated coplanarity value, i.e. measured height of the discrepancy between the connections and the calculated placement plane is entered in the representation of the features in Âµm.
To identify the features, you must zoom into the image.
