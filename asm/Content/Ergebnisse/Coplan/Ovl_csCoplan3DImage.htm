3D sensor: Topographical image

The measured heights are shown as grayscale values:

Grayscale value

The lighter the grayscale value, the closer the feature is to the sensor.

This display is used to assess the quality of the raw data from the 3D measurement. The zoom function can be used to better assess individual connections.

Depending on the scan resolution selected, the image can be shown compressed or elongated vertically.
