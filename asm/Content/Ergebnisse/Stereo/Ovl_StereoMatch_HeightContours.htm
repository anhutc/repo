Stereo measurement: Contour lines

2D image with contour lines in the camera image. This is used to check whether the features have been detected at the correct position.

The left and right stereo images are shown alternately.

The less the features move in the image, the more accurately they lie on the focal plane and are able to be detected easily.

Contour lines:

The contour lines are shown in different colors (e.g. cyan, blue, magenta). The gap between the contour lines corresponds to the vertical resolution.

Yellow

The tip of the pin is at the focal distance of the camera and can be detected clearly.

Red

The tip of the pin is too close to the camera.

Green

The tip of the pin is too far from the camera.

If the tips of the pins are shown in red or green, this is evidence of an error:

Check that the correct nozzle or gripper was used and that the height of the component has been entered correctly.
