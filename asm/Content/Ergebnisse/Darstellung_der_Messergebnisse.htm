Representation of the measurement results

In the various views available, the measurements are displayed which have been determined for each of the recognition steps carried out internally.

You can select the recognition steps shown in the list directly or using the following buttons:

the results of the next step or

the results of the previous step

 

 

Results of the recognition steps:

 

The recognition step returned OK (normal) - no color marking.

The recognition step failed, an error occurred.

The recognition step is an (error) result, but will be ignored during further processing.

 

+

Shows all the individual steps for this measurement step.

-

Only shows the parent measurement step. If at least one measurement step is marked red, the parent step is also marked red.
