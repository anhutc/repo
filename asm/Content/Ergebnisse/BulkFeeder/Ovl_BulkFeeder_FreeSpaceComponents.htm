SIPLACE BulkFeeder X: Clearance check

Only the components that have been detected as being individual components and are neither tilted nor flipped are highlighted here.

Red (with red border)

All components that are located at less than the entered minimum component distance from at least one other component.

These are highlighted as not detected and cannot be picked up.

Green (with green border)

All components that are located at more than the minimum component distance from all the surrounding components.
