SIPLACE BulkFeeder X: Detection of flipped components

When components are shaken out of the container, some of them may lie with their underside facing upward and cannot therefore be placed. These must not be picked up.

The vision system determines the brightness level (possible values: 0 = dark through 255 = light) in a rectangle in the center of the component. The value measured from below is then compared with a brightness threshold (default: 127) to determine whether the component was picked up with the underside facing upward, i.e. flipped.

This brightness threshold is either set manually or determined by teaching.

 

Default settings:

Brightness threshold = 127

The top of the component is darker than the underside.
