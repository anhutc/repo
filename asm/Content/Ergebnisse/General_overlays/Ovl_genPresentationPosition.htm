Recognition step: Presentation position

The model of a component, a fiducial, a component pocket, a nozzle or a barcode.

Yellow frame

Region in which the center of the object to be measured (component, fiducial, ink spot, component pocket, nozzle or barcode) must be located.

Yellow wedge

Maximum possible angle of rotation.

Green outline

Component recognition: Corresponds to the component body and any description of the features.

Component pocket recognition: corresponds to the dimensions of the component pocket.

Nozzles: Corresponds to the dimensions of the nozzle or the nozzle ducts.

Barcode: Corresponds to the specified size of the barcode.

The model of a substrate.

Yellow wedge

Maximum possible angle of rotation of the substrate.

Green circle

Expected external contour of the substrate.

Green lines

Connecting lines between evaluation points and the center of the substrate.

Individual gray shaded rectangles

Evaluation points for detecting the position of the substrate.

Multiple gray shaded rectangles

Position at which the notch is expected for determining the orientation of the substrate.

 

Only when glue dot detection is enabled:

Glue dot.
