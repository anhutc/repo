Recognition step: Y alternative dimension

This recognition is primarily used for tantalum components which may have different colored bodies.

If the edges of a component could not be detected by the "Y dimension" recognition step for a dark component, dimension checking is carried out by detecting the component connections.

Representation of the edge information (edges in Y direction):

Cyan curve

Representation of edge positions and edge quality in the form of a gradient.

The peaks in this curve indicate the grayscale transitions (indication of component edges).

Green lines

Detected edge positions.

If no green line is shown, the system was unable to detect the edges of the component or the dimensional tolerances of the component were exceeded.

Yellow lines

An edge of a component (green line) must lie between these lines.

Pink double-headed arrow

Orientation of gradient formation.

Gray rectangle

Area over which the gradient is generated (depends on component size, component tolerance and packaging tolerance).
