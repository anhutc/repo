Generating robustness figures

 

The robustness figures can only be generated when the underlying measurement was successful. If this is not the case, the Robustness button is disabled.

 

  1. Switch to the Robustness view by clicking the Robustness icon.

  2. Start the robustness analysis by clicking the Robustness analysis button.

  3. Evaluation of the results:
    The fewer measurements that are classified as failed and the more balanced the results are (the majority of the measurements flagged as successful in the middle of the range), the more reliable recognition of a component is.
    If a large number of measurements are flagged as not being recognized, you should attempt to improve the measurements by adjusting the parameters. Then repeat the robustness analysis in order to determine whether the results have improved or deteriorated.
