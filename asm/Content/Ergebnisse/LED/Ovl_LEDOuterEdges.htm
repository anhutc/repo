LED offset

If a surface feature has been described as a rectangle, all four edges are measured. The intersections of these edges result in a rectangle.

Orientation: Bottom (analogously for Top, Right and Left orientation)

Placement position: Center of the edge with which the component is aligned.

Green line

The edge with which the position is aligned.

Green cross

Placement position: Center of the edge with which the position is aligned.

Blue lines

Measured edges and center lines of the detected rectangle.

Orientation: Area centroid

The placement position is aligned with the area centroid.

Green cross

Placement position: Area centroid with which the position is aligned.

Blue lines

Measured edges and center lines of the detected rectangle.

Orientation: Vertex centroid

The placement position is aligned with the intersection of the center lines.

Green line

Measured center lines of the rectangle.

Green cross

Placement position: Intersection of the center lines.

Blue lines

Measured edges of the detected rectangle.

Orientation: Intersection of diagonals

The placement position is aligned with the intersection of the diagonals.

Green lines

Measured diagonals of the rectangle.

Blue lines

Measured edges of the detected rectangle.
