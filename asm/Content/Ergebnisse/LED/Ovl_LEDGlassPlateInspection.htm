Inspection of glass plate

Inspection to check whether there are any components still on the glass plate and whether the glass plate is dirty or damaged.

The inspection is started automatically and the stationary camera takes an image of the glass plate. This is inspected to identify any areas in the image that deviates significantly from the average grayscale value of their surroundings. These are then highlighted in red.

 

Prerequisite for performing the inspection:

The gantry must not be positioned above the stationary camera during measurement.

Restriction:

  • Only the region covered by the field of view of the camera, in which LEDs or dies can be placed, is inspected.
  • The minimum size at which soiling can be detected is 1 mm x 1 mm.

 

Regions highlighted in red

Soiling, damage or components have been detected on the glass plate.
