Recognition step: Fine position (position recognition)

The exact position of a feature (e.g. corner, pattern/contour) is determined.

Yellow rectangle

Search area in which the feature being searched for must be located.

Green rectangle

The center of the feature must be located in this area.

Yellow wedge

Angular tolerance of the feature.

Measurement step completed successfully:

Indicates the center of the feature which was used to describe the polygon.

Special case for D-Pack components: Center of the wraparound

Measurement step was not completed successfully: The fine position of the feature could not be determined correctly.

Filter point pairs
(yellow and dark blue points):

These must lie on the sides of the corners or on the contours of the feature that has been described as a "Pattern".

 

 

 

 

Light features:

Blue filter points

Must lie on the connections.

Yellow filter points

Must lie outside the connection surfaces.

Dark features:

Blue filter points

Must lie outside the connection surfaces.

Yellow filter points

Must lie on the connection surfaces.

Connection lines:

Yellow

The feature was able to be detected.

Red

Detection error.
