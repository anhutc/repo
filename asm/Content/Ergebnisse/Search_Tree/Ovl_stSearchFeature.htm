Recognition step: Features searched for (position recognition)

Display of the features used for position recognition:

A maximum of 16 leads or balls are inspected for position recognition.

Light blue
(with small features,
can only be seen in zoom mode.)

Corresponds to the feature description
(Gullwing lead: lead width, contact length,
J-Lead, wraparound: lead width, lead length,
balls: diameter of balls,
columns: diameter of columns)

Filter point pairs
(yellow and dark blue points):

These must lie on the sides of the corners or on the contours of the feature that has been described as a "Pattern".

 

 

 

 

Light features:

Blue filter points

Must lie on the connections.

Yellow filter points

Must lie outside the connection surfaces.

Dark features:

Blue filter points

Must lie outside the connection surfaces.

Yellow filter points

Must lie on the connection surfaces.

Edge points
(orange points)

If balls or columns are located very close to each other, no point pairs are shown. Instead, only points close to the edge of the balls / columns are shown.

Red frame

If the component is so large that the complete component does not fit into the field of view of the camera, only a portion of the component is inspected. This is indicated by the red rectangle.

Blue frame

Areas which have already been evaluated.

 

Problems:

  • The component is located outside the search area:
    Check the pickup position and the pickup tolerance specification (packaging tolerance in the programming system).
  • The yellow frame is only just larger than the component:
    Check the packaging tolerance and reduce the tolerances to the minimum necessary.
