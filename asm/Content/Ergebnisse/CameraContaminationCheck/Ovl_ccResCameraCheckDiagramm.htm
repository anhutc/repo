Results of checking the camera for soiling.

The results provided are the degree of soiling and the loss of brightness of the camera:

  • If the mean brightness in the dark areas exceeds a specified threshold, the camera is dirty and must be cleaned.

  • If the mean brightness in the light areas falls too much, the camera must be inspected (brightness calibration).

A value for "Cleaning is due" or "Camera verification is due" is shown for each illumination plane used.

Cleaning is due

Degree of soiling

 

No soiling or only slight soiling. Cleaning is not required.

 

Cleaning is recommended.

 

Fully clean the camera as instructed, irrespective of the level for which the degree of soiling is shown in the yellow or red range.

Camera verification is due
Loss of brightness

 

The camera is okay.

 

Medium loss of brightness. Brightness calibration is recommended.

The loss of brightness has exceeded the specified threshold:

Perform camera verification (brightness calibration).

 

Note:

The degree of soiling is measured separately for each illumination plane because soiling on the camera (e.g. beam splitter, beam splitter cube, lens, ...) will have a different effect on each illumination plane depending on the type of soiling and its location.
