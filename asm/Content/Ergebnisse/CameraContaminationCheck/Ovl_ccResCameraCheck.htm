Result: Camera soiling

Yellow rectangle

Region of interest for detecting if a camera is dirty.

The mean brightness is determined inside this region (excluding the yellow circles).

This region of interest corresponds to the black surface of the contrast tool.

Yellow circles

Region of interest for measuring whether camera verification is necessary.

These regions of interest correspond to the white surfaces of the contrast tool.

The mean brightness is determined in each of the regions of interest.

Restriction: The various regions of interest are not shown for multiple measurements.

 

Values determined when checking the camera for soiling:

Loss of brightness

It is recommended that a camera should be cleaned and/or inspected as soon as one value is shown in yellow.
