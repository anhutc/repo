Shows the result of the soiling inspection under the specified illumination.

The contrast tool is first positioned in front of the camera in the orientation in which it is picked up and is then rotated through 90 Â°:

This makes it easier to identify soiling on the contrast tool itself, thus avoiding misinterpretation in the event that the contrast tool is dirty or scratched. The two images are displayed alternately.

This additional test is not performed when a board camera is being inspected.

 

Note: If the contrast tool is positioned in front of the camera several times, the various results are presented at the same time.
