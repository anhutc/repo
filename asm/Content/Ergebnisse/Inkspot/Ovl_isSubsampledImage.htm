Recognition step: Subsampled image - taught ink spot

Representation of the camera image at reduced resolution. Each n x n pixels of the original image are merged to a single image in the subsampled image.

The parameter n is specified during teaching. Subsequent calculations are always carried out with the subsampled images (the training images are also subsampled).
