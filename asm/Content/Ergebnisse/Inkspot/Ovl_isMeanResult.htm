Recognition step: Classification by brightness method (mean grayscale value)

 

Example:

Grayscale value area with green outline

For one mean grayscale value in this area, a "go" decision was taken ("place").

Grayscale value area with red outline

For one mean grayscale value in this area, a "no-go" decision was taken ("do not place").

Blue bar

The actual mean grayscale value of the region of interest.

In the example, the decision was "go", i.e. "place".

If the blue bar is located between the areas with green and red outlines, the result cannot be clearly assigned by the system.

 

The background indicates the mean grayscale value.
