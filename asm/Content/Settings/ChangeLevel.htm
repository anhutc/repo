Changing the activity level

To quickly change to a higher activity level:

  1. Click a button showing the following icon: .

  2. Then choose the required higher level, enter the corresponding password and click OK.

Changing to a different activity level:

  1. Opens the Settings > User settings view:

  2. Click the button for the required activity level.

  3. Enter the password for the selected activity level and click OK.
    Only when switching to "Production" level is it not necessary to enter a password.

The setting is applied and all views are now adjusted to the selected level.
