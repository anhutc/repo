Creating a bug report at any time

Bug report: Detailed machine information at a given time.

How to create a bug report:

Press the keyboard shortcut <Ctrl><Alt><F10>.

Alternatively:

Touch one corner of the screen with your finger and move your finger diagonally to the opposite corner.

When you remove your finger, a popup menu appears, allowing you to create a bug report.

 

The bug report is stored under:

C:\Sirio\BugReports\archive\Log_DateTime.zip
