Glass plate for measuring LEDs

In order to measure the exact position of LED components, a glass plate is fitted to the stationary camera SST 47. The LEDs are placed on this plate and first measured from below. The active regions of the component are then measured from above using the board camera.

This step is necessary to allow the components to be placed as precisely as possible, taking into account the active regions. A setting can also be made in SIPLACE Vision specifying whether rotational offset of the active regions in relation to the component should also be taken into account.

The glass plate is constructed in such a way that the bottom of the LEDs is on the focal plane of the stationary camera.

Camera: Flip chip camera (SST 47) - Stationary component camera for measuring the relative positions of the top and bottom sides of the component
