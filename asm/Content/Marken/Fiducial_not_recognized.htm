Fiducial not recognized

Proceed as follows if a geometrically described fiducial is not recognized:

  1. Check the fiducial description
    Check all sizes specified together with the relevant tolerances.
  2. Check the illumination values
    - Is the fiducial well illuminated?
    - Can the fiducial be easily distinguished from the background?
  3. If the fiducial still cannot be recognized, you must optimize the algorithm parameters:
  • Contrast threshold
    If the contrast between the fiducials and the background is poor, the threshold can be reduced.
    It is also possible that other structures will be recognized as fiducials.
  • Subsampling
    Reduce the subsampling level if the fiducial can no longer be clearly distinguished in the image.
  • Edge quality
    Increase this value if the edges of the fiducial to not form a regular line or circle.
    At the lowest quality level, (value = 5), the detected points are no longer tested for plausibility.
  • Distance between points
    The edges of the fiducial must always be between the points of a point pair.
    Is the tolerance of the fiducial (position or size) greater than the distance between the points?
