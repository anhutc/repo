Flash find

During teaching, and on the basis of a pattern for a fiducial, this algorithm calculates the model used to measure the PCB fiducials during placement.

A fiducial should only be taught if the geometrical nature of a fiducial prevents it from being described geometrically.

 

Advantage:

  • Any shape fiducial is possible

Disadvantage:

  • The lower the quality of the fiducial during teaching, the worse the model and the lower the recognition rate.

  • The reference point must be placed precisely in order to ensure the highest possible placement accuracy. Inaccuracies can arise in particular if this reference point is placed manually.
