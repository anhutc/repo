Options for describing fiducials and ink spots

There are 2 options for describing fiducials and ink spots:

  • Description of the geometrical form - Definition of a synthetic fiducial or synthetic ink spot
  • Teaching - Comparison with a model of the fiducial or ink spot

 

Recommendation:
If it is possible to describe fiducials and ink spots geometrically, i.e. if they have a defined geometrical shape, this method should be selected in order to achieve the best possible results.
