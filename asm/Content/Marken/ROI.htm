ROI Region of interest

The area of the image used for creating a model.

 

How to specify the region of interest:

 

  1. Click the Region of interest button or
    click any parameter that describes the region of interest.
  2. A cross is shown in the image:
    Draw the region of interest around the fiducial by clicking in the window and then moving your finger over the touchscreen or moving the mouse pointer over the image while holding the mouse button down (the position at which you pressed the left mouse button indicates the top left corner and the position at which you released the left mouse button indicates the bottom left corner).

 

 

Hint:
Flash find, template method and histogram method:

The region of interest should be selected in such a way that it is a little larger than the structure which is to be recognized.

 

Mean grayscale value:

The region of interest should be completely within the structure.
