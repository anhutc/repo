What method for what ink spot?

When selecting an evaluation method, you should make sure that the features which are evaluated by the methods can be clearly classified into "go" and "no-go".

 

Recommendation:

 

"Go" - place

 

 

Fiducial: defined structure with a lot of structure information

PCB structure: pads, traces, etc.: high contrast

Homogeneous area of the board (generally light), very low contrast.

 

 

 

"No go" -

Do not place


Covered with sticker: Low contrast, grayscale depends on color, can vary
