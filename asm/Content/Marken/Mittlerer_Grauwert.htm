Mean grayscale value

The mean brightness of the region of interest is determined from the training images for the "go" case and the "no-go" case. Then the system sets two brightness thresholds which demarcate the "go" and "no-go" areas.

Advantage:

  • Very fast
  • No defined form required

Disadvantage:

  • Sensitive to fluctuations in brightness
    Use of this model is problematic in lines with different PCB camera brightnesses
  • Position recognition must be performed before ink spot recognition
