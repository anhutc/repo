Problems when evaluating ink spots

The ink spot is detected for faulty ("bad") panels or boards and the panel or board is assembled:

Check whether the entire structure is well covered and the ink spot does not shine through.
Remedy: Change the algorithm parameters to reduce their tolerance.

 

Also check that the correct side was masked off. If the masking point has slipped, the system measures next to the masking point.

 
The evaluation time is too long:

The complete camera image always has to be evaluated with geometrically described ink spots.
With very small structures (e.g. 0.3 mm), the evaluation time is long, because only a small amount of subsampling is possible.

Remedy: Switch to taught ink spots if the long evaluation time is unacceptable.
