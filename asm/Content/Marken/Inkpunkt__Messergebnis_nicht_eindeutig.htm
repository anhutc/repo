Taught ink spots: Measurement results are not clear

During placement, the ink spot of a panel is compared with the models stored during teaching ("go" case and "no-go" case).

 

If the system cannot clearly decide whether the inks spot identifies a panel which is to be assembled ("go" case) or a panel which is to be omitted ("no-go" case), you must decide what action is to be taken for the panel during the teaching operation.
