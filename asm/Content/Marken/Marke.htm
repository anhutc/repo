Fiducials

 

After a PCB has entered the center conveyor or processing conveyor and before placement is started, the exact position of the PCB is determined by means of the PCB vision system. This is necessary so that any misalignment and shearing of the PCB can be taken into account in the exact calculation of the placement positions.

For PCB position recognition, reference fiducials are applied to the PCB which are then measured by the PCB camera. At least two fiducials must be present so that the system can detect any deviations in the position and rotation angle. Three fiducials are required to detect any signs of compression and distortion of the PCB and the PCB layout.

Before a fiducial can be used for PCB position recognition it must be geometrically described. If it is not possible to describe a fiducial on the basis of its geometrical shape, the system must "learn" the fiducial by means of a measurement process - the fiducial is taught.

Advantages of geometrical description:

- no falsification of the model as a result of noise in the camera image during teaching ("training the image").

- reference point is always set to the ideal position.
