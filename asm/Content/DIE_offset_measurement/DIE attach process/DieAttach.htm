Die attach process

The die is placed in such a way that the top of the die on the board corresponds to the top of the die on the wafer.

To do this, it is picked up with the flip unit, rotated, passed to the die attach unit, rotated further and then picked up by the placement head.

The die is picked up with a segment of the flip unit.

The flip unit is rotated through 65°.

The die attach unit then takes over the die on the underside.

The die attach unit is rotated through -115° and the component is picked up by the placement head.

The die is placed on the board with the top facing upwards.
