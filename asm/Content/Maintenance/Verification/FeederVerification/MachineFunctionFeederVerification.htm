Maintenance > Verification > Feeder verification

You can use this verification step to test correct mechanical operation of a feeder. Any offsets determined during teaching are not taken into account.

Prerequisite for performing verification: the machine must be calibrated.

Start

Takes a camera image of the pickup area of the selected feeder.

Feed forward (2 mm)

If the center of the component pocket is not positioned at the marker for the pickup position set in the feeder, press this button to step the belt forwards by the smallest possible increment.

Feed forward (current pitch)

Press this button to check whether the next component is moved to the pickup position correctly.

Restart

Takes a new camera image of the pickup area of the selected feeder.

Close

Closes the dialog box.
