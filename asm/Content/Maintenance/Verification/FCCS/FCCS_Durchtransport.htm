Transporting a plate through the machine during camera calibration

If camera calibration is to be performed on one machine in a line, it is possible to specify that the other machines are to transport the camera verification plate through the machine without performing any calibration.

Prerequisites for performing illumination calibration or transporting the plate through individual machines:

  • The machine must have been referenced and calibrated.

  • The middle lane must have been removed on machines with 3 lanes.

 

This moves the camera verification plate through a machine without calibrating the cameras:

  1. Ensure that there are no more boards in the machine and that there are no more components on the heads.

  2. Switch to the Maintenance view and press Calibrate illumination.

  3. Make sure that no camera is selected.

  4. Press the Next button.

  5. Ensure that all the prerequisites displayed are met.

  6. Press the Start button.
