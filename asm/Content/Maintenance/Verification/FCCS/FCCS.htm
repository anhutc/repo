Maintenance > Camera verification

The digital cameras of the SIPLACE Vision system have illumination facilities which may be subject to aging and soiling in daily operation. To ensure correct image recognition, it is necessary to perform brightness calibration (illumination calibration) at long, regular intervals.

Purpose of camera verification (FCCS - Field Camera Calibration System): The camera gain and the illumination factors for each illumination level are to be calibrated. The shading correction factors are NOT determined again, as it can be assumed that they do not change over time.

A special plate with calibration tools is used for calibrating the camera. This is placed in lane 1 of the machine.

Objective: The values are to be set in such a way that the camera image of a standardized calibration tool reaches a predefined brightness with the specified illumination values.
