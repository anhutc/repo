Transmission error rate for the VBI - VHI connection: DPM

 

DPM

Defects per million

DPM = 1

1 data packet of 1 million transfered data packages was errored.

DPM = 0

There were no transmission errors. The value is not displayed.

DPM ≥ 1

There was at least one transmission error. The value is displayed.

DPM ≥ 100

Indicates a transmission problem:

 

•

Check the connectors of the trailing cable between the VBI and the VHI.

 

•

If necessary, replace the VBI or VHI.
