Circuit breakers and logic signals

Circuit breakers:

Green

Circuit breaker is OK. Voltage is > 80% of the rated voltage.

Red

Voltage < 80% of the rated voltage.

Gray

Circuit breaker is not attached.
 

Hardware fault: The connection between the station and the circuit breaker board has been interrupted:
Make sure that the cable between the IOCU and the circuit breaker board of the power supply is connected correctly and that the cable and connectors are in perfect working order.

The short names of the circuit breakers correspond to the captions in the image of the corresponding circuit breaker board.

 

Logic signals for CSB - contactor-based safety breaker: The CSB ensures that the output voltages are switched off reliably.

Green

The signal is present.

Red

The signal is not present.

Voltage drop counter

Number of voltage drops since the last reset.

Reset voltage drop counter

All counters are reset to zero.

Color key for the individual power supply units:

A voltage drop has occurred for at least one circuit breaker.

At least one circuit breaker has failed (voltage < 80%).
