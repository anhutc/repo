SMPS - Switched Mode Power Supply: This generates all the DC output voltages (300 V, 160 V, 42 V, 27 V and 24 V) for the SIPLACE placement machines from the customer's power supply network.

This central power supply consists of switched components that use AC/DC converters to transform the 3-phase AC supply into the DC output voltages needed for the SIPLACE placement machines.
