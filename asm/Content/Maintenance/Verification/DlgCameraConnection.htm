Maintenance > Verification > Camera connections

The GigE camera system allows the status of the connection between the computer and the camera to be determined, provided that the VBI and VHI versions are 02 or later. If a fault status is detected for a camera, you can open the detail view and identify the faulty section of the connection. Always check all components in this context.

Example: If the connection is interrupted between the VBI and the VHI, the problem could lie with the VBI, the trailing cable or the VHI.

Note: These actions can only be performed on certain machines fitted with GigE camera systems.

Connections

PC - VBI

Red GigE Vision LAN cable between the GigE interface card and the Vision Base Interface.

VBI - VHI

Trailing cable between the Vision Base Interface and the Vision Head Interface.

Displays the transmission error rate if at least one in a million data packages transferred between the VBI and the VHI was errored.

VHI - camera

Green or black cable with gray connector between the Vision Head Interface and the camera.

VBI - camera

Red GigE Vision LAN cable between the Vision Base Interface and the camera.

PC - camera

GigE Vision LAN cable between the GigE interface card and the SIPLACE BulkFeeder X camera.

Position

Location to which the camera is assigned.

Status

OK: All the connections between the computer and the camera are okay.

Faulty: At least one connection between the computer and the camera is faulty. Press to show details on the error.

Unknown: The cameras are unavailable and the VBI or VHI is not responding.

Interrupted: The connector is not plugged in.

Actions
