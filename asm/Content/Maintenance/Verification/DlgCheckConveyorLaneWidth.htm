Maintenance > Verification > Conveyor rail verification

The objective is to use the camera images to identify whether the conveyor rails of a conveyor lane are secured parallel to each other in the processing area.

The lines at intervals of approx. 1 mm are used for orientation purposes.

Actions

Capture images

The board camera is used to take images at certain positions along the conveyor rail in the processing area of the selected lane.

Use the images to check whether the conveyor rails are parallel along their entire length.

If this is not so, the conveyor rails must be screwed in place correctly.

When this has been done, capture a new set of images and check the result.

Generate report

Creates a report documenting the results of all the individual steps carried out to date. You can enter your name and edit or add to the existing comment.

The report is saved under:

VerificationConveyorRails_<MachineType>_<MID>_<Date>_<Time>.pdf

You can view the reports under LineDiagnostics in the programming system.
