Machine verification

Checks placement accuracy.

Subsystem verification

Placement head verification

Checks the function and state of the placement heads.

Camera verification (illumination calibration)

Illumination calibration: To ensure correct image recognition, it is necessary to perform illumination calibration at regular intervals.

Camera connections

Queries the status of the connection between the PC and the camera (on machine types with GigE camera system).

Conveyor rail verification

The camera images are used to identify whether the conveyor rails of a conveyor lane are secured parallel to each other in the processing area.

Conveyor motor verification

This function can be used to check that the conveyor is moving uniformly.

Power supplies

SMPS - Switched Modular Power Supply.

Feeder verification
(only shown for certain machine types)

You can check individual feeders to establish whether they would position components at the pickup position sufficiently accurately when the feeder is stepped forwards.

CAN bus resistance verification

The resistance values at CAN bus 1-4 are measured.

Leveling assistant

Height measurement process that is able to mechanically measure the planarity of a selected surface.

Check for camera soiling

Determines the degree to which a component camera is soiled and the extent to which it is necessary to carry out camera verification.
