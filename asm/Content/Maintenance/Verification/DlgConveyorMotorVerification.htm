Conveyor motor verification

The objective is to verify correct functioning of the conveyor by determining the motor current values:

•

Belt motors

Determines the motor current when the conveyor belts are moved backwards and forwards.

•

Lifting table motors

The lifting table is slowly raised and lowered and the reference point of the motor is determined.

•

Width adjustment motors

Determines the motor current value for the spindles and the width adjustment facility of the right/left conveyor rail and the zero point correction.

 

Actions

Verify conveyor motors

The individual motor current value is determined, saved to the corresponding parameters and shown as a bar chart. If the value lies outside the specified range, the indicator mark is in the red section.

Generate report

Creates a report documenting the results of all the individual steps carried out to date. You can enter your name and edit or add to the existing comment.

The report is saved under:

VerificationConveyorMotors_<MachineType>_<MID>_<Date>_<Time>.pdf

You can view the reports under LineDiagnostics in the programming system.
