Leveling a surface

Proceed as follows to level a surface in the machine:

1.  Switch to the view Maintenance > Verification > Leveling assistant .

2.  Select the surface you require from the Available surfaces.

3.  Press Start.

4.  Adjust the set screws to compensate for the results shown for the individual measuring points.

5.  Press Start again to repeat height measurement.

6.  Repeat steps 4 and 5 until the surface is as level as possible.
