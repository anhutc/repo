Predictive maintenance

Overview of the maintenance steps to be carried out: The higher the maintenance due status, the sooner you should carry out the relevant maintenance.

Hardware component

The maintenance due status is currently shown for

- CPP heads and C&P20 heads

- the X and Y axes

- the vacuum pump

- the tape cutter

Maintenance due status

 

 

Specifies when maintenance should be carried out on the component shown.

0%: Maintenance has just been carried out..

100%: Maintenance must be carried out.

> 100%: Maintenance is overdue and must be carried out urgently.

At a maintenance due status of 80%, the entry for the corresponding component and the higher-level entry are shown in orange.

The button also appears in orange.

Selected item

Reset maintenance due status

Press this button to reset the value to 0 % when maintenance has been performed.
