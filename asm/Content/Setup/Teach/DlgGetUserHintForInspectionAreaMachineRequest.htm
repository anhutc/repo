Actions after teaching an inspection area

A teach request requires that an inspection area has to be learned again. Choose what action is to be performed after the teaching operation:

Inspect this area

Starts inspection of the area that has just been learned.

Place panel without inspection

Starts assembly of the panel. The area that has just been learned is not inspected again.

Skip panel without inspection

The panel is not assembled. No inspection is performed.

Skip component without inspection

The component will not be placed. No inspection is performed.

Deactivate inspection for this area

Inspection will no longer be performed for this area of the board.

 

Close

Closes the dialog box.
