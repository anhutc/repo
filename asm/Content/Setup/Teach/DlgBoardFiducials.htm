Functions

Position board

The board is moved in such a way that the selected fiducial is in the center of the camera image.

Remove boards

The board is moved out of the machine.

Test selected fiducial

Launches SIPLACE Vision and measures the selected fiducial.

Test board

The board in the selected conveyor is moved through the machine automatically. All the fiducials intended for board recognition are measured.

Actions (Only appears after the test procedure has started.)

Cancel testing

Cancels the current operation and switches to the previous view.

Inspect defective fiducial

Starts the manipulation views in SIPLACE Vision to check the fiducial.
