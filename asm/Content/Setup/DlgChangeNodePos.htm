Functions > Position adjustment

If a component pocket is not located completely in the field of view of the camera, this view allows the gantry to be positioned manually.

Positions

Offset relative to original position:

Offset of the position of the component pocket in X and Y from the originally assumed position.

Absolute position

Shows the current coordinates of the component pocket in X and Y relative to the machine zero point and the alignment of the component pocket.

Directions

Every click of these buttons moves the gantry one step of the selected step size in the corresponding direction.

Specify the step size.

First click this button and then click a position in the camera image. The camera is moved to the corresponding position.

Control

Reset position

Sets the coordinates back to the original position.

 

Caution:

Changes to the position made here only relate to the display in SIPLACE Vision and to the teaching process. The pickup position is not adjusted.
