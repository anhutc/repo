Tray editor: Outline

Define the position and size of the tray (external dimensions):

When this button is pressed, use the dial on the right-hand side to move the top outer edge of the tray and use the dial below the image to move the left outer edge.

When this button is pressed, use the corresponding dial to enlarge or reduce the outline.

You can also enter the values manually:

1

Top

Distance between the top edge of the tray to the zero point of the tray carrier.

2

Right

Distance between the right edge and the zero point of the tray carrier.

3

Left

Distance between the left edge and the zero point of the tray carrier.

4

Bottom

Distance between the bottom edge and the zero point of the tray carrier.

5

Tray length

Size of the tray in X.

6

 

Tray width

Size of the tray in Y.
