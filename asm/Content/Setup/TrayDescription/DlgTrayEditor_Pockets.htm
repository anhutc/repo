Tray editor: Pockets

Define the position and size of the pocket arrangement:

When this button is pressed, use the dial on the right-hand side to decrease or increase the size of the margin between the top row of pockets and the top edge of the tray (1) and use the dial below the image to decrease or increase the size of the margin between the left-hand column of pockets and the left-hand edge of the tray (2).

When this button is pressed, use the corresponding dial to decrease or increase the size of the margin between the right-hand column of pockets and the right-hand edge of the tray (3) and between the bottom row of pockets and the bottom edge of the tray (4).

You can also enter the values manually:

1

Tray margin at top

Distance between the top row of pockets and the top edge of the tray

2

Tray margin at left

Distance between the left row of pockets and the left edge of the tray.

3

Tray margin at right

Distance between the right row of pockets and the right edge of the tray.

4

Tray margin at bottom

Distance between the bottom row of pockets and the bottom edge of the tray.

5

Pocket length

Horizontal size of the pocket (in X).

6

 

Pocket width

Vertical size of the pocket (in Y).
