Automatic feeder pitch detection

The aim is for the machine to automatically determine the actual distance between the centers of component pockets.
To do this, the feeder pitch is gradually increased from the lowest setting during the initial pickup operations from a feeder. As soon as the machine is able to pick components without errors, the current pitch is set.

Proceed as follows to perform automatic feeder pitch detection:

  1. Switch to the view Setup > X-Table , select the track you require and press Settings.

  2. Choose the setting "Auto detect" from the Feeder pitch drop-down menu.

Prerequisites for automatic detection:

    -   8 mm or 2 x 8 mm X-Feeder

    -   The facility for checking the presence of the component using the component sensor or vacuum checking must be enabled in SIPLACE Pro.
