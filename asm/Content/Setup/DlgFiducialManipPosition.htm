Board view > Fiducial position adjustment

Positions

Position relative to fiducial

Shows the current fiducial position in X and Y relative to the specified position for the fiducial.

Position relative to board:

Shows the fiducial position in X and Y relative to the coordinate system of the board. Any rotation of the fiducial will also be shown.

Directions

Every click of these buttons moves the gantry one step of the selected step size in the corresponding direction.

Specify the step size.

First click this button and then click a position in the camera image. The camera is moved to the corresponding position.

Save

The current position is saved.

Control

Reset position

The most recently saved position is accepted.
