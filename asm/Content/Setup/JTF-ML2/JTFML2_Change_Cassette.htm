Multiple JEDEC Tray Feeder: Replacing the cassette or tray / removing the cassette

Replacing the cassette or an individual tray:

1.  Switch to the view Setup > Locations > X-Table , select the JTF-ML2 feeder and press Settings...

2.  In the Refill position drop-down menu, select Move to refill position for the cassette to be replaced.

3.  Open the loading door.

4.  Pull out the cassette and replace it completely or replace individual trays as specified.

5.  Push the cassette back into the tower.

6.  Close the loading door.

7.  In the Refill position drop-down menu, choose Quit refill position.

Removing a cassette:

1.  Switch to the view Setup > Locations > X-Table , select the JTF-ML2 feeder and press Settings...

2.  In the Refill position drop-down menu, select Move to refill position for the cassette to be replaced.

3.  Open the loading door.

4.  Pull out the cassette.

5.  Close the loading door.
