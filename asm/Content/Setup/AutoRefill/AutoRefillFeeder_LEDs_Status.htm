AutoRefillFeeder 8mm X: LEDs, status indicators and function keys

Pitch indicator:

The illuminated LED shows the pitch currently set.

In this example: 4 mm.

Menu display:

Default.
A submenu has been selected.

Function keys:

Forward

Initially selects the tape duct: .

Tape is inserted:

• Pressing briefly moves the tape forward by one pitch increment.

• Pressing and holding moves the tape from the selected tape duct to the pickup position.

If no tape is inserted:

• Pressing and holding turns the pinwheel forward

Back

Initially selects the tape duct: .

Tape is inserted:

• Pressing briefly moves the tape backward by one pitch increment.

• Pressing and holding moves all tapes or the selected tape out of the feeder: Remove tape.

If no tape is inserted:

• Pressing and holding turns the pinwheel backward

 

Selects the tape duct:

Press once briefly: Tape duct 1 is selected (LED 1 of the pitch indicator flashes rapidly).

Press twice briefly: Tape duct 2 is selected (LED 2 of the pitch indicator flashes rapidly).

In combination with the Set button : Allows parameters (pitch, speed, ...) to be set.

Sets a parameter

(e.g. pitch, speed).

Status indicator of the feeder:

The feeder is required for the current job and at least one tape duct is ready.

 

 

Warning.

More information is available from the pitch indicator LEDs:

The supply voltage is too low when the feeder is switched on.

 

Error.

More information is available from the pitch indicator LEDs:

CAN bus error, e.g.:

• The fiber optic cable of the Feeder Control Unit EDIF (contactless power and data interface) is dirty-
• There is a foreign body between parts of the EDIF and the feeder.
• The feeder is not fully pushed home.

Check whether the EDIF interface is dirty or whether any foreign bodies are trapped and clean if necessary.

Make sure that the feeder is pushed home fully.

Transport errors:

The tape reel is jammed in the container.

Eliminate the reason for the jam and press the Forward button after selecting the tape duct.

The tape could not be moved to the pickup position. It is possible that components are jammed between the tape and the pickup window in the pickup area.

Press the tape duct selection button briefly (once for tape duct 1, twice for tape duct 2) and press and hold the Back button .

The tape must then be removed, cut and inserted again.

The drive for the front pinwheel is blocked.

The toothed wheel is jammed by components.

Check the toothed wheel and remove any components.

Then press the Forward button on the feeder to check the drive.

The refill drive for the top tape duct is blocked.

 

Press the tape duct selection button twice briefly and then press the Back button briefly.

The refill drive for the bottom tape duct is blocked.

 

Press the tape duct selection button once briefly and then press the Back button briefly.

 

Forward chasing light

Press the removal handle home.

The stored factory data is invalid (the machine readable feeder ID and the pickup position offset are no longer available).

Send the feeder in for repair.

Update the software.
