AutoRefillFeeder 8mm X - Cutting the tape

Note the following when cutting a tape in order to ensure that a tape can be picked up and moved forward by one of the rear pinwheels in the AutoRefill feeder:

  • Cut the tape at a point at which the foil has not yet been separated.

  • Cut the tape between 2 holes so that a complete hole remains in which the pinwheel can engage and that the distance to the hole is as large as possible.

Example: The hole shown in orange is the first hole at which the foil has not yet been separated.

Green line: Cutting is permitted.

Red lines: Cutting is not permitted (where possible the cut should not be made through a component pocket and not too close to the first hole).

Tip:

Use the cutting unit for the SIPLACE AutoRefillFeeder 8mm X.
