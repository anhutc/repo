Reject plate

Components that could not be placed as a result of an error during Vision measurement are deposited on this plate.

This is necessary for expensive components in particular, as they would otherwise be dropped into the reject bin if no component reject conveyor is fitted. To ensure that components are deposited on the component reject conveyor or on the reject plate, you must check the "Use reject device" box in the programming system.

The aim is to be able to place the components at a later time. You can use the relevant Vision image to decide whether to attempt to place the component again. To do this, replace the component at the specified pickup position.

However, if the component is clearly faulty, dispose of it by hand.
