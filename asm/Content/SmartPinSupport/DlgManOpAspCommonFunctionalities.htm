Pin picker functions

Air kiss

On / Off

The air kiss on the pin picker is activated or deactivated.

Magnetic force

On / Off

The magnetic in the base of the pin is activated or deactivated.

Z axis

Move down to reference position

The Z axis of the pin picker is moved down at a particular position in the machine where there is no lower limit.

Move down (Only displayed for the Service activity level.)

For testing the mechanical unit: The pin picker is moved to the bottommost position. The pin picker is moved back up after the dialog box that appears has been acknowledged.

Measurements

Measurement values

Updates the display to show the new values that have been determined.
