fortunecookie
Clear application's cache from the storage
settings or by 3D-Touching an application.

Modern Succession

fortunecookie is the modern successor of CacheClearer, originally created by Ryan Petrich and modified by julioverne.

Still Functioning

fortunecookie still has the original feature, which adds two buttons in the storage settings. Simply select an application and you'll see two options: Clear Cache and Reset App.

More Practical Than Ever

fortunecookie now has a new feature. Easily clear an application's cache simply by 3D-Touching/Force Touching the application's icon in your homescreen.

You can also disable this implementation or it's haptic feedback and/or alert in the preferences pane.
